## Goals

### Short-term (This Quarter)

- Produce a GSD-ready refactor baseline from the incoming v2 docs: requirements, acceptance criteria, conflicts, and canonical references.
- Use [[MarkOS v2 GSD Master Work Plan]] as the pre-discuss artifact before any v2 implementation planning.
- Plan the next GSD step around remaining implementation decisions: F-contract IDs, agent token namespace, connector depth, and phase insertion.
- Treat public MarkOS pricing as `{{MARKOS_PRICING_ENGINE_PENDING}}` until the Pricing Engine creates cost-aware, market-aware, approved recommendations.

- Treat SaaS Marketing OS strategy as a future post-suite destination map until a GSD translation prompt creates planning artifacts.
- Preserve v4.0.0 SaaS foundations while identifying the smallest complete operating loop that proves the new product thesis.

### Medium-term (This Half)

- Ship the first complete MarkOS operating loop: brand pack, connector wow moment, agent run substrate, task board, approval inbox, content brief/draft/publish/measure path, and learning instrumentation.
- Establish Tenant 0 as the production dogfood workspace for MarkOS marketing.
- Make artifact performance and literacy freshness first-class data so the product starts learning from day one.

### Long-term (This Year+)

- Evolve MarkOS into the AI-native marketing operating system that replaces fragmented agency/tool workflows with orchestrated, evidence-backed, human-approved agent loops.
- Grow toward tenant-specific literacy overlays and anonymized cross-tenant learning without breaking tenant isolation or human review.
- Build a product moat around brand intelligence depth, sourced research, performance instrumentation, and operator trust.

## Aspirations

_What kind of engineer/person am I becoming?_

- A product engineer who turns large strategy documents into precise, testable operating constraints.
- A system designer who protects trust, evidence, and reversibility while still moving quickly.
- A builder who can keep the vault, roadmap, and codebase in conversation instead of letting them drift apart.

## Anti-goals

_What am I explicitly NOT optimizing for?_

- Building all 80 envisioned agents before one complete loop works end to end.
- Passive dashboards that do not help an operator decide or act.
- External-world agent mutations without approval gates, provenance, cost controls, and rollback paths.
- Central literacy updates that bypass human review.
- Contract or token churn that breaks existing v4.0.0 SaaS foundations.
