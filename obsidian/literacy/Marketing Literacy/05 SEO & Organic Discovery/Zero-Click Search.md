# Zero-Click Search

> A dominant share of search queries in 2026 end without a click — the SERP or LLM answer resolves the intent. The marketing job shifts from "rank and get the click" to "be *present* in the answer."

## Why zero-click keeps growing

- **AI Overviews / AI Mode** show a full answer at the top.
- **SERP features** — People Also Ask, featured snippets, knowledge panels, instant maps, price boxes, shopping carousels.
- **Answer engines** — ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude, Copilot resolve entire research tasks without returning the user to Google.
- **Mobile voice assistants** — Siri, Google Assistant, Alexa give one answer.
- **On-device AI** — Apple Intelligence, Copilot+ PC, Android AI answer locally without a SERP.

## Strategic responses

1. **Brand entity presence** — be the cited source in the answer. See [[Generative Engine Optimization]] and [[Entity SEO]].
2. **Own the instant answer** — structured data, tight opening paragraphs, clean comparison tables.
3. **Capture assisted demand downstream** — measure lift in branded search, direct traffic, app opens, CRM inquiries rather than session-level clicks.
4. **Invest in brand channels** that are click-native — email, SMS, push, retargeting, creator content, private community.
5. **Shift measurement** from last-click CTR to share-of-voice in AI answers, branded-query volume, and unaided awareness.

## Content patterns that win zero-click

- Definition-first pages with a 1-sentence lede + 50–200-word body.
- Tabular comparisons for `X vs Y` queries.
- Schema-rich FAQ pages (but only when the questions are genuinely the user's).
- Long-tail data pages where no single competitor has the number.
- Original research with one citable stat per page.

## Pitfalls

- Panic: removing content so "at least they click." Absent content = absent brand, not more clicks.
- Obsessing over CTR on queries that are structurally zero-click (definitions, weather-like questions, simple conversions).
- Measuring only the click, ignoring the view (impression share, AI-citation share).
