# Ad Message Crafting

> Channel: **ad**. Stage 6 of [[Message Crafting Pipeline]]. Every draft in this channel conforms to the rules below + brand voice from [[Brand System Canon]].

## Constraints

- Headline ≤ 30 chars main; variants 15/25/30.
- Description: one-sentence proposition + one proof hint.
- Creative: brand-consistent LoRA; AI-gen labelled per platform + EU AI Act.
- Compliance: specific claims sourced to claim library.
- Frequency cap set; fatigue-aware rotation.

## Canonical structure

1. Hook headline — pain or promise
2. Description — specific proof
3. Visual — one idea, thumb-stop
4. CTA — verb + specific outcome
5. Disclosure — AI + comparison claims

## Hook patterns

- direct claim
- pattern interrupt
- social proof stat
- category redefinition

## Proof patterns

- specific %, $, × metric
- brand logo row
- case study thumbnail

## Anti-patterns (auto-reject)

- vague superlatives
- CTA-less creative
- brand-logo-as-copy
- stock photo + bold text

## Voice defaults

Blunt on X/Meta; professional on LinkedIn; dynamic on TikTok.

## Audience × pain tailoring

Every draft composes: **this channel's canonical structure × resolved [[Audience Archetype Canon|archetype]] × tagged pain from [[Pain-Point Engine]] × brand voice from [[Brand System Canon]] × optional triggers from [[Neuro Audit Canon]]**.

## Rejection criteria

- Missing archetype or pain tag → reject (Canon commandment I + II).
- Violates constraints above → reject or revise.
- Fails voice classifier or claim check → reject.
