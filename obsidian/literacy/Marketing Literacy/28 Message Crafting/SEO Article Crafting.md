# SEO Article Crafting

> Channel: **SEO / long-form**. Stage 6 of [[Message Crafting Pipeline]]. Every draft in this channel conforms to the rules below + brand voice from [[Brand System Canon]].

## Constraints

- H1 includes target entity + intent.
- First 200 chars = standalone declarative answer (GEO bait).
- Schema.org (Article + FAQPage where applicable).
- E-E-A-T signals: named author, sources, date, update cadence.
- Internal links to cluster + atomic nodes.

## Canonical structure

1. H1 — entity + intent
2. Declarative lede (GEO-quotable)
3. TL;DR box / summary
4. Body — H2/H3 hierarchy, one idea per section
5. Proofs — quotes, stats, diagrams
6. FAQ section with schema
7. Conclusion with next steps
8. Related (internal) + further reading (external)

## Hook patterns

- definitive answer in first sentence
- contrarian thesis

## Proof patterns

- original research data
- expert quotes
- named case studies

## Anti-patterns (auto-reject)

- AI-boilerplate openings ("In today's fast-paced world…")
- thin content + FAQ schema stuffing
- unsourced claims

## Voice defaults

Professional, authoritative, direct. See [[Communication Guides]] reading level by audience.

## Audience × pain tailoring

Every draft composes: **this channel's canonical structure × resolved [[Audience Archetype Canon|archetype]] × tagged pain from [[Pain-Point Engine]] × brand voice from [[Brand System Canon]] × optional triggers from [[Neuro Audit Canon]]**.

## Rejection criteria

- Missing archetype or pain tag → reject (Canon commandment I + II).
- Violates constraints above → reject or revise.
- Fails voice classifier or claim check → reject.
