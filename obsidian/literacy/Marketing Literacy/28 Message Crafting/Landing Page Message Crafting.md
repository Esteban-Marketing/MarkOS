# Landing Page Message Crafting

> Channel: **landing page**. Stage 6 of [[Message Crafting Pipeline]]. Every draft in this channel conforms to the rules below + brand voice from [[Brand System Canon]].

## Constraints

- H1 ≤ 10 words; promise specific.
- Sub-head ≤ 20 words; proof or differentiator.
- First 100 words answer who · what · why-now · why-you.
- Social proof in first viewport.
- One primary CTA; secondary for hesitant.
- Mobile-first; one-thumb nav.

## Canonical structure

1. Hero — H1 + sub-head + primary CTA + hero image/video
2. Proof row — logos, quote, count
3. Problem section — named, empathic
4. Solution section — specific, demonstrable
5. Features → outcomes — not features alone
6. Objection handling — FAQ or comparison
7. Repeat CTA in context
8. Footer — legal, links, alt CTA

## Hook patterns

- specific outcome + time frame
- pain articulation
- category anchor

## Proof patterns

- customer quote + photo + role
- metric with context
- logos bar
- awards + badges

## Anti-patterns (auto-reject)

- feature wall
- stock-photo hero
- multi-CTA competition
- hidden pricing

## Voice defaults

Confident, specific, empathic — expressive and warm.

## Audience × pain tailoring

Every draft composes: **this channel's canonical structure × resolved [[Audience Archetype Canon|archetype]] × tagged pain from [[Pain-Point Engine]] × brand voice from [[Brand System Canon]] × optional triggers from [[Neuro Audit Canon]]**.

## Rejection criteria

- Missing archetype or pain tag → reject (Canon commandment I + II).
- Violates constraints above → reject or revise.
- Fails voice classifier or claim check → reject.
