# Social Post Message Crafting

> Channel: **social**. Stage 6 of [[Message Crafting Pipeline]]. Every draft in this channel conforms to the rules below + brand voice from [[Brand System Canon]].

## Constraints

- Platform-native length + format.
- Hook in first 2 seconds / lines.
- Caption + visual aligned.
- Disclosures (creator + AI) when applicable.
- One clear takeaway per post.

## Canonical structure

1. Hook — line 1 or first 2 seconds
2. Context — what + why-now
3. Insight — single idea
4. Proof or story — specific
5. CTA (optional) — save / share / comment / click

## Hook patterns

- contrarian take
- personal story
- specific result
- question echoing audience pain

## Proof patterns

- case study micro
- data point
- screenshot
- in-app metric

## Anti-patterns (auto-reject)

- engagement-bait questions
- buzzword soup
- repost without platform adapt

## Voice defaults

LinkedIn professional; X blunt; TikTok personal; Threads conversational.

## Audience × pain tailoring

Every draft composes: **this channel's canonical structure × resolved [[Audience Archetype Canon|archetype]] × tagged pain from [[Pain-Point Engine]] × brand voice from [[Brand System Canon]] × optional triggers from [[Neuro Audit Canon]]**.

## Rejection criteria

- Missing archetype or pain tag → reject (Canon commandment I + II).
- Violates constraints above → reject or revise.
- Fails voice classifier or claim check → reject.
