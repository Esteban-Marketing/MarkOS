# Email Message Crafting

> Channel: **email**. Stage 6 of [[Message Crafting Pipeline]]. Every draft in this channel conforms to the rules below + brand voice from [[Brand System Canon]].

## Constraints

- Subject 30–60 chars; preview 50–100; body per type (transactional 50–150, nurture 150–300, long 300–500).
- Plain-text parity.
- One primary CTA, optional secondary if orthogonal.
- SPF/DKIM/DMARC aligned; BIMI optional.
- Unsubscribe link + physical address (CAN-SPAM).

## Canonical structure

1. Subject — one hook: curiosity OR specificity
2. Preview — complements, does not repeat
3. Opening line — name the reader's situation
4. Body — 1 pain acknowledged + 1 promise + 1 proof + 1 CTA
5. Sign-off — consistent sender identity

## Hook patterns

- personal context
- provocative stat
- question echoing pain
- direct claim
- name-drop authority

## Proof patterns

- customer quote
- metric with context
- case study micro-snippet
- expert reference

## Anti-patterns (auto-reject)

- "I hope this finds you well"
- AI clichés
- multi-CTA spray
- emoji overuse
- sender lookalike spam

## Voice defaults

Conversational, direct, warm for nurture; cooler for cold.

## Audience × pain tailoring

Every draft composes: **this channel's canonical structure × resolved [[Audience Archetype Canon|archetype]] × tagged pain from [[Pain-Point Engine]] × brand voice from [[Brand System Canon]] × optional triggers from [[Neuro Audit Canon]]**.

## Rejection criteria

- Missing archetype or pain tag → reject (Canon commandment I + II).
- Violates constraints above → reject or revise.
- Fails voice classifier or claim check → reject.
