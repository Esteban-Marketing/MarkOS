# Retention & Churn Playbook

> Parent tag: `poor_retention_churn`. Routing: Lifecycle Email · Product-Led Growth.

## Symptoms (diagnose)

- Net MRR churn > 1% monthly (SaaS) or > 3% (consumer subscription)
- Repeat-purchase rate < 20% (e-com)
- 30-day active users falling at constant signups
- Support tickets rising on onboarding steps

## Root causes (below the symptoms)

- Activation failure — users never hit the "aha" moment
- Value dilution — pricing/packaging out of alignment with usage
- No lifecycle nurture after activation
- Feature drift — product evolves faster than user education
- Competitor pressure — easier, cheaper, or better alternatives

## Treatments (what to do)

1. Map activation event; instrument rigorously
2. Build lifecycle flows: welcome → onboarding → feature-discovery → expansion → winback
3. Health scoring — leading indicators (logins, depth, collaboration)
4. Success-manager or support intervention at risk thresholds
5. Reverse trial or downgrade flow to preserve LTV at pricing friction
