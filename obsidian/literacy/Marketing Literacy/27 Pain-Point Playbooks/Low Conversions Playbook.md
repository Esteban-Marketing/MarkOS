# Low Conversions Playbook

> Parent tag: `low_conversions`. Routing: Paid Media · Landing Pages.

## Symptoms (diagnose)

- Ad → LP CTR okay but LP → lead < 2.5% (B2B) / 4% (B2C)
- Form-started → submitted gap > 40%
- Cart abandonment > 70%
- Trial signup → activated < 30%

## Root causes (below the symptoms)

- Message-market mismatch: ad promised X, LP delivered Y
- Friction — form length, load time, trust gaps
- Weak proof — no testimonials, social proof, guarantees
- Wrong CTA — asking for too much too early (full demo vs lightweight trial)
- Mobile experience broken — 60%+ of traffic but CVR half of desktop

## Treatments (what to do)

1. Message match audit — ensure ad promise = LP H1
2. Friction tear-down — remove fields, add autofill, clarify next step
3. Add social proof in first viewport (logos, quotes, counts)
4. Swap primary CTA to lower-commitment step (calculator, audit, email course)
5. Mobile-specific LP; one-thumb navigation

Friction removal, trust, clarity. Proof: before/after, testimonial. CTA: self-serve trial.

## Success criteria

- LP CVR increased ≥ 25% vs baseline
- Form completion rate > 75%
- No uptick in bad-fit leads downstream

Instrument via [[Unified Measurement]]. Baseline before treatment. Expected change window 30–120 days per treatment.
