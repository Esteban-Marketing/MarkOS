# Content Engagement Playbook

> Parent tag: `content_engagement`. Routing: Social · Content/SEO.

## Symptoms (diagnose)

- Social posts < 0.5% engagement rate
- Newsletter CTR < 2%
- Blog time-on-page < 60 seconds
- Zero UGC or reshares

## Root causes (below the symptoms)

- Hook failure — first 2 seconds or 2 lines do not stop the scroll
- Wrong format for platform (long-form on TikTok, short on LinkedIn)
- Content matches vanity keywords, not audience pain
- Distribution-only strategy without community seeding
- No distinctive POV — vanilla vs. differentiated take

## Treatments (what to do)

1. Hook rewrite sprint — A/B 5 hooks per piece
2. Platform-native reshape; do not repurpose unchanged
3. Pain-first topic selection — content that solves a concrete issue
4. Seed in 3–5 relevant communities first; amplify on public social
5. POV editor — every piece has a take, not a summary

- Time-on-page > 2 minutes on flagship posts
