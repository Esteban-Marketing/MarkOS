# Pipeline Velocity Playbook

> Parent tag: `pipeline_velocity`. Routing: Lifecycle Email · Sales Enablement.

## Symptoms (diagnose)

- Opp-aging > 60 days in mid-stage
- Nurture open + reply rates decaying over a sequence
- MQL → SQL conversion < 10%
- Reps complain leads are unqualified

## Root causes (below the symptoms)

- Weak nurture — generic, rhythm-only, no pain matching
- No handoff SLA between marketing and sales
- Missing mid-funnel content (comparison, ROI, integration)
- No lead scoring updates; sales works stale list
- Unclear next step in each message — no micro-commit

## Treatments (what to do)

1. Rebuild nurture by pain-tag; one sequence per dominant pain
2. Set lead-routing SLA; escalation rules for stalled opps
3. Add BOFU content: ROI calculator, case studies, implementation guides
4. Re-instrument lead scoring with product-usage + intent data
5. Every email includes one concrete next step
