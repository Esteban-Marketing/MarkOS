## MarkOS Pricing Engine

In MarkOS v2, pricing literacy is operationalized through [[Pricing Engine Canon]]:

- Cost model before pricing recommendations.
- Competitor pricing intelligence and price-history diffs.
- Business-type modules for SaaS, eCommerce, and Services.
- Pricing recommendations with projected impact, risk, evidence, and test design.
- Approval gates for price changes, price tests, packaging changes, and public pricing page edits.

Active MarkOS price points use `{{MARKOS_PRICING_ENGINE_PENDING}}` until the Pricing Engine produces approved recommendations.

[[SaaS Marketing OS Strategy Canon]] adds pricing-sensitive SaaS growth use cases: upgrade prompts, save offers, referral incentives, affiliate commissions, G2 pricing profile sync, pricing-page experiments, and in-app conversion nudges. These all remain Pricing Engine-owned.
