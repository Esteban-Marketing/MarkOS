# Reference Example: **B2B** (HR Technology — Applicant Tracking)

---

## Phase Budget & Parameters
- **Target CAC Limits:** $1,200 (blended, all paid channels)
- **Target CPA Bounds:** $280 per MQL; $1,200 per SQL
- **LTV Goal:** $18,000 ARR per closed customer
- **Daily Spend Threshold:** $350 (scale to $600 after 14-day ramp validation)

## 1. Audience Matrix

**Primary Targeting (LinkedIn Campaign Manager):**
- Job Titles: "HR Director", "VP People", "Head of Talent Acquisition", "Chief People Officer", "Recruiting Manager"
- Company Size: 50–500 employees (avoid <50 — too small to need ATS; avoid >500 — procurement cycle too long)
- Industry Exclusions: Staffing agencies, government, non-profit (different buying motions)

**Retargeting Layers:**
- Visitors to `/pricing` or `/demo` in last 30 days → priority warm outreach (SDR sequence, not just ads)
- Webinar attendees in last 90 days → "You attended our session on X — here's what customers see after 90 days" creative angle
- Email list upload (existing leads) → LinkedIn-matched CRM retargeting

**Lookalike Seeds:**
- Current customers who closed in <60 days (fastest conversion path)
- MQL list with lead score >80

## 2. Pipeline Execution: LinkedIn Ads (Primary Channel)

**Sprint 1 — Top of Funnel (Problem-Aware):**
- Format: Single image + carousel
- Angle 1: Insight post-style ad — "HR teams like yours spend 7 hours/week manually scheduling interviews" (pain quantification)
- Angle 2: Benchmark data — "Mid-market companies using ATS hire 2.4x faster. Here's the data."
- CTA: "See the research" → gated PDF/report → MQL capture
- Budget: 60% of LinkedIn budget
- KPI: CPL <$80, CTR >0.8%

**Sprint 2 — Middle of Funnel (Solution-Aware):**
- Format: Video testimonial (60 seconds) featuring a named HR Director and specific hire-rate metric
- Angle: "How [Company X] reduced time-to-hire by 38% in 90 days"
- CTA: "Request a personalized demo"
- Budget: 30% of LinkedIn budget
- KPI: Demo request rate >2.5%, CPL <$240

**Sprint 3 — Bottom of Funnel (Evaluation/Decision):**
- Format: LinkedIn Message Ads (InMail) to re-engaged leads who haven't booked
- Copy: Concise, specific to their company size — reference their job postings if visible
- CTA: "30-minute no-obligation audit of your current hiring workflow"
- Budget: 10% of LinkedIn budget
- KPI: Reply rate >8%, booking rate >3%

## 3. Pipeline Execution: Google Ads (Intent Capture)

- **Branded Defense:** Exact match on company name + [company name + pricing/alternative/review]. $40/day.
- **Competitor Terms:** "Greenhouse alternative", "Lever vs [Company]", "best ATS for mid-market" — lower CPCs, high intent. Bid $4–$8 CPC.
- **Problem-Intent Keywords:** "how to reduce time to hire", "manual interview scheduling tool", "applicant tracking system 50-250 employees". Drive to a comparison guide, not homepage.
- **Negative Keywords:** "free ATS", "ATS for enterprise" (>5000 employees), "ATS for staffing agency"

## 4. Optimization Loop

- Review at Day 7: Kill any ad variant with CTR <0.4% on LinkedIn or CPC >2x target on Google
- Review at Day 14: Pause lowest 20% of audience segments by CPL; reallocate budget to top 2
- Review at Day 21: If pipeline quality is good but volume is low → expand company size range to 40–600; if quality is poor → tighten job title targeting

## Quick Wins (First 14 Days)

1. **Upload customer list to LinkedIn** as a Lookalike seed before spending any budget on cold audiences
2. **Run the competitor keyword campaign on Day 1** — lowest CPCs, highest intent, fastest pipeline signal
3. **Gate a "Hiring Benchmarks Report"** as TOF lead magnet — better conversion than a demo request for cold traffic

## Channels to Avoid (For Now)

- Meta/Instagram: B2B HR buyers are not in purchase mode on consumer social
- TikTok: Not applicable for $1,200 CAC product
- YouTube Pre-roll: Long production cycle, hard to A/B test at this stage
