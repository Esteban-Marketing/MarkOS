> Community is the discipline that converts customers into advocates and strangers into leads
> through the gravity of belonging. A community is not an audience — an audience watches,
> a community participates. Communities reduce CAC over time because the community sells for you.
> They require sustained human investment. Do not activate without the capacity to maintain them.
