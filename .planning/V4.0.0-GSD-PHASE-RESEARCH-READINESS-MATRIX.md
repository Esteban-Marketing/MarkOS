## Non-Negotiable Gates

- No hard-coded public pricing strategy before approved PricingRecommendation records.
- No external price, package, discount, save offer, billing-provider, social publish, support reply, or outreach mutation without approval and audit.
- No public claim without EvidenceMap or strict evidence audit support.
- No future target agent becomes executable until AgentRun v2, cost, approval, evidence, UI/API/MCP, and tests are defined.
- No connector-dependent agent acts on stale, missing, or degraded connector data without a recovery task and human-visible blocked state.
- No cross-tenant learning promotion without anonymization, sample-size gate, and admin review.
- No SaaS Suite phase claims doc 17 growth modules as implemented; Phases 214-217 only provide foundation and extension points.
- No phase 218-220 growth module becomes customer-facing or externally mutating without Pricing Engine, evidence, approval, AgentRun, and SOC2 controls.
- No commercial-engine phase creates a new silo for identity, consent, communication, analytics, pricing, or approval truth.
- No provider-specific lock-in is allowed without a replaceable adapter contract, export posture, and migration path.
- No future launch, email, messaging, partner, or referral mutation path may exist outside shared task, approval, evidence, and audit controls.
