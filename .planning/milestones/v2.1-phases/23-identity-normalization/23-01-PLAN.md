# Plan 23-01: Identity Audit & Classification

## Wave 1 — Classify Remaining MARKOS Surfaces

<task id="1" name="Inventory identity-bearing surfaces">

    - historical-only

  <acceptance_criteria>

    - Future migration targets are deferred deliberately, not implicitly
  </acceptance_criteria>
</task>

  <acceptance_criteria>

  </acceptance_criteria>
</task>
