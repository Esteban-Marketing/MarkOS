# Plan 215-05 - Billing Approvals, Corrections, Dunning, and Evidence

**Phase:** 215 - SaaS Billing, Payments, and Multi-Country Compliance  
**Status:** Planned  
**Requirements:** SAS-05, TASK-04, COMP-01, QA-01..15

## Objective

Keep billing mutations controlled and auditor-ready.

## Scope

- Define approval requirements for invoice correction, refund, credit, discount, write-off, dunning override, and failed-payment recovery.
- Link decisions to billing events, subscription state, evidence, and audit log.
- Preserve read-only access during billing holds where appropriate.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Billing mutations cannot bypass approval and audit.
- Hold/recovery evidence remains visible.
- Tenant-facing billing copy uses translated labels while preserving raw lineage behind evidence.

## Verification

- Approval and evidence tests cover billing correction, refund, discount, and failed sync recovery.
