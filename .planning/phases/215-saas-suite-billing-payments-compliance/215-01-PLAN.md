# Plan 215-01 - SaaS Invoice and Billing Event Contracts

**Phase:** 215 - SaaS Billing, Payments, and Multi-Country Compliance  
**Status:** Planned  
**Requirements:** SAS-04, SAS-05, BILL-01, COMP-01, QA-01..15

## Objective

Define invoice, payment, dunning, and billing event objects for SaaS subscriptions.

## Scope

- Define `SaaSInvoice`, `SaaSPaymentAttempt`, `SaaSBillingEvent`, dunning state, refunds, credits, and corrections.
- Link invoices to subscriptions, plans, PricingRecommendation, processor IDs, accounting state, and evidence.
- Add audit, RLS, retention, and export posture.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Invoice records support commercial and legal billing needs.
- Corrections, refunds, credits, discounts, and write-offs are approval-aware.
- Pricing fields use Pricing Engine-approved values or placeholder.

## Verification

- Contract tests cover invoice lifecycle, correction, refund, credit, and approval-required actions.
