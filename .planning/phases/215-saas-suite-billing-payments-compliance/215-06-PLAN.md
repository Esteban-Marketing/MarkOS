# Plan 215-06 - Future Incentive Payout Compliance Hooks

**Phase:** 215 - SaaS Billing, Payments, and Multi-Country Compliance  
**Status:** Planned  
**Requirements:** SG-04, SG-06, SG-11, SG-12, PRC-09, QA-01..15

## Objective

Reserve compliant hooks for future referral, affiliate, partner, and incentive payouts without implementing growth modules now.

## Scope

- Define placeholder payout policy references for referral rewards, affiliate commissions, partner payouts, and incentive experiments.
- Require Pricing Engine context, approval, tax/legal posture, fraud controls, and audit before future payout activation.
- Keep all future payout objects non-runnable until post-217 GSD translation.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Future payout work cannot bypass pricing, billing compliance, approval, audit, or tax posture.
- No referral/affiliate/partner module becomes active in Phase 215.
- Requirements are documented for post-217 planning.

## Verification

- Non-activation checks cover future payout modules and target agents.
