<threat_model>
- T-93-05 (provider blind failure): missing API keys or timeouts could crash the pipeline and violate DRT-09. Mitigation: every adapter must emit auditable skipped, degraded, or error attempts and allow the route to fall back safely.
- T-93-06 (authority dilution): external snippets could be treated as equal to approved internal evidence. Mitigation: normalize an explicit authority_class and preserve the internal-first ranking hints from the policy layer.
- T-93-07 (unsafe crawl breadth): Firecrawl or Tavily could fan out too broadly and violate D-01. Mitigation: allow targeted URL and allow-list driven extraction only; no always-on broad crawling.
- T-93-08 (schema drift across providers): citation, freshness, or implication data could be lost in provider-specific wrappers. Mitigation: convert every provider result into one evidence schema before merge time.
</threat_model>
