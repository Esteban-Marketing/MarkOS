# Plan 211-01 - Strategy and Brief Object Model

**Phase:** 211 - Content, Social, and Revenue Loop  
**Status:** Planned  
**Requirements:** LOOP-01, LOOP-02, PRC-09, EVD-01..06, QA-01..15

## Objective

Define the strategy and brief records that start the first complete operating loop.

## Scope

- Model objective, audience, pain tag, offer, channel, proof requirements, pricing context, success target, and owner.
- Link briefs to AgentRun, EvidenceMap, PricingRecommendation or placeholder, and future artifact performance logs.
- Define required fields for content, social, and revenue-oriented briefs.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Every brief has audience, pain, offer, evidence requirements, and measurable target.
- Pricing-sensitive briefs use Pricing Engine context or `{{MARKOS_PRICING_ENGINE_PENDING}}`.
- Briefs can feed draft generation and approval.

## Verification

- Contract fixtures cover content, social, SaaS, and pricing-sensitive briefs.
