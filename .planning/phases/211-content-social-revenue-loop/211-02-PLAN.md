# Plan 211-02 - Draft Generation and Audit Pipeline

**Phase:** 211 - Content, Social, and Revenue Loop  
**Status:** Planned  
**Requirements:** LOOP-03, EVD-02, TASK-04, QA-01..15

## Objective

Move artifacts through generation and audit before any approval or dispatch.

## Scope

- Define MarketingArtifact and ArtifactAudit records.
- Run voice, claim, compliance, channel-fit, pricing, and evidence audits.
- Route unsupported or risky artifacts to edit/reject/research tasks.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Drafts are channel-native and auditable.
- Unsupported claims block approval until fixed or labeled.
- Audit output links to EvidenceMap and approval payloads.

## Verification

- Tests cover passing, failed, pricing-blocked, and evidence-blocked audit cases.
