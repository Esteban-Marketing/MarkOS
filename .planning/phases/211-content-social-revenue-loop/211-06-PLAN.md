# Plan 211-06 - Measurement Handoff to Learning and Weekly Narrative

**Phase:** 211 - Content, Social, and Revenue Loop  
**Status:** Planned  
**Requirements:** LOOP-08, LRN-01, LRN-02, QA-01..15

## Objective

Close the first operating loop by handing outcomes to learning surfaces.

## Scope

- Write expected performance envelopes before dispatch.
- Capture measured outcomes, attribution evidence, and lesson candidates.
- Feed Weekly Narrative and Learning/Literacy phase inputs.
- Create next-task recommendations from outcome deltas.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Every dispatched artifact has expected and actual outcome slots.
- Measurement creates learning or next work, not only charts.
- Weekly Narrative can cite the loop's source records.

## Verification

- End-to-end loop fixture covers brief, draft, audit, approval, dispatch, measurement, and learning handoff.
