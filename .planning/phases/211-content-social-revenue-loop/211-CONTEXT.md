# Phase 211 Context - Content, Social, and Revenue Loop

**Status:** Seeded from the 2026-04-22 incoming 00-17 discussion review.

## Why this phase exists

MarkOS must prove one complete marketing operating loop: strategy -> brief -> draft -> audit -> approval -> dispatch -> measure -> learn. The current app has useful parts, but the vault requires a coherent loop with evidence, Pricing Engine context, social/revenue signals, approvals, and learning.

Doc 17 makes this phase especially important because future SaaS growth modules depend on the same operating loop discipline.

## Required phase shape

1. Define strategy and brief objects tied to audience, pain tag, offer, pricing context, proof requirements, and success target.
2. Define draft generation and audit pipeline for voice, claims, compliance, channel fit, pricing, and evidence.
3. Define approval-to-dispatch path for at least one channel.
4. Define social signal schema and escalation posture.
5. Define revenue attribution feedback through CRM/pipeline or leading indicators.
6. Define measurement handoff to ArtifactPerformanceLog and Weekly Narrative.
7. Keep doc 17 PLG/ABM/referral/community/event/PR/partnership/developer modules as future consumers of the loop.

## Non-negotiables

- No public content, reply, DM, ad change, CRM mutation, data export, or price claim without approval or earned-autonomy record.
- No pricing-sensitive artifact without Pricing Engine context or `{{MARKOS_PRICING_ENGINE_PENDING}}`.
- No factual claim without EvidenceMap support or inference label.
- No measurement-only reporting that does not update tasks, narratives, learning, or strategy.
- No doc 17 growth module implementation in this phase; only loop compatibility and first proof.

## Done means

GSD can plan one real operating loop that makes MarkOS credible as a marketing OS before expanding into SaaS growth modules.
