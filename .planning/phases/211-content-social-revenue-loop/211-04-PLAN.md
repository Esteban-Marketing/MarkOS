# Plan 211-04 - Social Signal Schema and Escalation

**Phase:** 211 - Content, Social, and Revenue Loop  
**Status:** Planned  
**Requirements:** LOOP-05, CONN-01..06, TASK-01, QA-01..15

## Objective

Prepare social listening, inbox, comment, DM, and escalation signals without unsafe automation.

## Scope

- Define SocialSignal records for comment, mention, DM, share, sentiment, urgency, revenue relevance, and approval need.
- Route social signals to tasks, CRM records, or approval inbox.
- Define which social actions are read-only, task-only, or approval-gated.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Social signals are classified and routed, not dumped into a dashboard.
- Replies, DMs, posts, and moderation actions are approval-gated by default.
- Revenue-relevant signals can link to CRM or pipeline evidence.

## Verification

- Fixtures cover positive, negative, urgent, sales, support, and spam signals.
