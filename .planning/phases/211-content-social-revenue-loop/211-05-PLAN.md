# Plan 211-05 - Revenue Attribution Feedback

**Phase:** 211 - Content, Social, and Revenue Loop  
**Status:** Planned  
**Requirements:** LOOP-06, LOOP-07, BILL-01, QA-01..15

## Objective

Tie content and social actions to revenue or leading indicators.

## Scope

- Link artifacts to UTM/campaign touches, CRM activity, pipeline movement, outbound events, or leading indicators.
- Define degraded attribution states when data is incomplete.
- Feed evidence into weekly narrative and performance logs.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Revenue impact is evidence-linked or explicitly marked degraded.
- CRM/pipeline signals can attach to artifacts and campaigns.
- No revenue claim is made without source context.

## Verification

- Attribution tests cover direct, assisted, degraded, and missing-data cases.
