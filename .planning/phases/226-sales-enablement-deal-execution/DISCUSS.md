# Phase 226 - Sales Enablement and Deal Execution (Discussion)

**Milestone:** v4.2.0 Commercial Engines 1.0  
**Depends on:** Phases 205, 209, 221, 222, 225  
**Quality baseline applies:** all 15 gates

## Goal

Add battlecards, objection intelligence, proof packs, proposal support, win/loss capture, and approval-aware deal execution tied back to CRM, Pricing Engine, and evidence.

## Scope

- Battlecards, proof packs, and competitor objection handling.
- Proposal and quote support tied to pricing truth and evidence freshness.
- Deal-room style execution support for opportunities and renewals.
- Win/loss and objection signal capture for learning.
- Sales-material governance across UI, API, and MCP.

## Non-Goals

- Core CRM workspace belongs to Phase 222.
- Analytics semantic layer belongs to Phase 225.
- Ecosystem partner-led co-sell systems belong to Phase 227.

## Discuss Focus

- Battlecard freshness and claim-safety rules.
- Proposal/quote boundaries vs Pricing Engine ownership.
- How objections, risks, and proof should flow into deal execution.
- What sales-facing surfaces need approval by default.

| 226-02 | Proposal, quote-support, and pricing-safe sales materials |
