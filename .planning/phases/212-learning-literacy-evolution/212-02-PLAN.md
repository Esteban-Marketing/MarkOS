# Plan 212-02 - TenantOverlay Learning Records

**Phase:** 212 - Learning and Literacy Evolution  
**Status:** Planned  
**Requirements:** LRN-03, EVD-01..06, QA-01..15

## Objective

Store tenant-specific lessons without changing central literacy.

## Scope

- Define TenantOverlay with lesson, discipline, evidence, confidence, expiry, provenance, review status, and suppression reason.
- Apply overlays only inside the tenant's context.
- Define expiry and review cadence.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Tenant overlays are RLS-protected and tenant-local.
- Every overlay has evidence and confidence.
- Stale overlays expire or require review.

## Verification

- RLS, expiry, confidence, and provenance tests are specified.
