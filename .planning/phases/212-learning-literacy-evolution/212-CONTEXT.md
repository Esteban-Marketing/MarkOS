# Phase 212 Context - Learning and Literacy Evolution

**Status:** Seeded from the 2026-04-22 incoming 00-17 discussion review.

## Why this phase exists

MarkOS must learn from artifact outcomes without leaking tenant data. The vault requires tenant-specific overlays, central literacy update candidates, anonymized aggregate learning, confidence scoring, admin review, and provenance.

This phase converts the first operating loop from Phase 211 into a learning system and prepares future SaaS growth modules to improve safely.

## Non-negotiables

- No tenant identifiers or PII in cross-tenant learning.
- No central literacy promotion without admin review and sample-size/confidence evidence.
- No tenant overlay without provenance and expiry/review posture.
- No experiment learning that bypasses EvidenceMap and approval.
- No "AI learned this" claim without measurable outcome and source trail.
