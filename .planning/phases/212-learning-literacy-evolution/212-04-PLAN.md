# Plan 212-04 - Cross-Tenant Anonymization and Privacy Controls

**Phase:** 212 - Learning and Literacy Evolution  
**Status:** Planned  
**Requirements:** LRN-04, COMP-01, QA-11, QA-15

## Objective

Prevent tenant data, PII, and proprietary tactics from leaking into shared learning.

## Scope

- Define anonymization, aggregation, sample-size, consent, and redaction rules.
- Classify tenant data that must never be promoted centrally.
- Add review gates for pricing, customer, support, and campaign details.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Cross-tenant learning has no tenant identifiers or PII.
- Sample-size and confidence gates are enforced.
- Sensitive examples require redaction before review.

## Verification

- Privacy tests cover PII, customer names, pricing, support text, and proprietary campaign examples.
