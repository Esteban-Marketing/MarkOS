# Plan 205-03 - Pricing Intelligence Store and Watch List

**Phase:** 205 - Pricing Engine Foundation + Billing Readiness  
**Status:** Planned

## Objective

Store own, competitor, market, and strategic pricing knowledge as quality-scored, versioned records.

## Scope

- Add Pricing Knowledge Object write/read/history/diff behavior.
- Add competitor watch list records with source URL, cadence, extraction method, source quality score, confidence, and legal/compliance posture.
- Detect meaningful changes in prices, packages, feature gates, value metrics, trials, discounts, and free tiers.
- Emit pricing alerts without making autonomous external changes.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Raw scrape/extraction text is not used as the product surface.
- Every pricing insight has source, timestamp, method, SQS, and confidence.
- Change detection distinguishes major, minor, and cosmetic changes.
- Alerts are tenant-isolated and auditable.
