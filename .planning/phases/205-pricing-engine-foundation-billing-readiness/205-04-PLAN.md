# Plan 205-04 - Pricing Recommendations and Price Tests

**Phase:** 205 - Pricing Engine Foundation + Billing Readiness  
**Status:** Planned

## Objective

Create operator-facing PricingRecommendation and PriceTest records with explicit approval gates.

## Scope

- Generate recommendation records with current state, market context, cost context, options, projected impact, risk, confidence, evidence, assumptions, and decision state.
- Add price-test design with control/test configuration, sample size, duration, metrics, stop conditions, rollback, and results.
- Require explicit approval before price-test activation, public price changes, packaging changes, discount strategy, or pricing page changes.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Recommendation output separates evidence from inference.
- PriceTest activation cannot happen without an approval decision.
- Rejected/deferred/modified decisions preserve reason and actor.
- Tests cover approval bypass denial and rollback metadata.
