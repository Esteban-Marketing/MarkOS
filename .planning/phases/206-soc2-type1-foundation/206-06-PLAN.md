# Plan 206-06 - Incident Response, BCP, DR, and Pen-Test Readiness

**Phase:** 206 - SOC 2 Type I Foundation  
**Status:** Planned

## Objective

Prepare operational controls for Type I and early enterprise scrutiny.

## Scope

- Incident response playbooks for agent misfire, connector credential leak, pricing error, data export error, billing error, and webhook delivery incident.
- Backup and disaster recovery evidence.
- Business continuity tabletop.
- Pen-test scope and remediation workflow.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Each critical incident class has owner, severity, communication path, rollback, and evidence requirements.
- DR/BCP tests produce auditor-ready evidence.
- Pen-test findings create tracked remediation tasks.
