# Plan 206-04 - Connector Privacy, Vendor, and Retention Review

**Phase:** 206 - SOC 2 Type I Foundation  
**Status:** Planned

## Objective

Control the connector layer that powers the v2 wow loop and recovery system.

## Scope

- Connector scopes, credentials, refresh tokens, encryption, revocation, and retention.
- Nango/direct adapter decision evidence.
- Vendor/subprocessor inventory for connector providers, LLMs, hosting, billing, observability, email, payment processors, accounting providers, support tools, product analytics tools, and DIAN providers.
- Connector failure and recovery evidence.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Each connector has approved scopes, data classification, retention, and revocation path.
- Direct adapters require explicit justification.
- SaaS Suite vendors such as Stripe, Mercado Pago, QuickBooks, Siigo, Alegra, support, product analytics, and DIAN-related providers have data classification and subprocessor posture.
- Dependent-agent pause/recovery behavior is auditable.
