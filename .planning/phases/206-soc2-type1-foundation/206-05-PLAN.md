# Plan 206-05 - Automated Evidence Collection

**Phase:** 206 - SOC 2 Type I Foundation  
**Status:** Planned

## Objective

Automate SOC2 evidence collection from MarkOS-owned logs and control surfaces.

## Scope

- Audit log, AgentRun events, approval decisions, billing ledger, webhook evidence, connector recovery, SaaS Suite billing/support/product/revenue evidence candidates, and policy acknowledgements.
- Evidence export format for auditor review.
- Coverage dashboard or report.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Evidence collection covers at least 95% of planned controls.
- Evidence can be exported without leaking tenant data across boundaries.
- Evidence model can cover SaaS Suite legal billing, support approvals, processor/accounting sync, and product usage processing before Phases 214-217.
- Missing evidence creates tasks, not silent audit gaps.
