# Plan 206-02 - AI Governance and Approval Controls

**Phase:** 206 - SOC 2 Type I Foundation  
**Status:** Planned

## Objective

Document and automate evidence for MarkOS human approval, agent action, model use, and external mutation controls.

## Scope

- Approval-required action families.
- AgentRun policy metadata, tool policy, model/provider use, and cost/budget controls.
- External mutation evidence for content publish, social replies, email, ads, CRM, data export, and price changes.
- Low-risk auto-approval policy requirements for later earned autonomy.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Approval bypass is a control failure with evidence.
- Model/tool use is traceable to tenant, actor, policy, and run.
- External-world mutation controls are covered by policy and test evidence.
