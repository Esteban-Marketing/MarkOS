# Plan 206-03 - Pricing Engine and Billing Control Mapping

**Phase:** 206 - SOC 2 Type I Foundation  
**Status:** Planned

## Objective

Ensure Pricing Engine, billing readiness, and downstream SaaS Suite billing decisions are covered by auditable controls.

## Scope

- PricingRecommendation and PriceTest approval evidence.
- Public pricing page and discount-strategy change controls.
- Billing usage ledger, pricing snapshot, and invoice evidence.
- Competitor pricing source governance and source-quality review.
- SaaS Suite future controls for save offers, discounts, subscription lifecycle mutations, refunds, credits, write-offs, and legal invoice corrections.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Price changes and price tests require approval evidence.
- Billing can prove which pricing recommendation/snapshot influenced a charge.
- Scraped/extracted competitor data has compliance and source-quality controls.
- SaaS Suite billing actions have a documented approval/evidence model before Phases 214-217 execute.
