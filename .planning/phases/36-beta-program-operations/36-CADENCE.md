# Phase 36 Cadence: Weekly Beta Operations Rhythm

## Weekly Operating System

Three recurring operating blocks define beta execution quality.

## Monday: Pipeline Review

- Owner: Intake Ops Lead
- Duration: 45 minutes
- Inputs:
  - New accepted intakes
  - Lifecycle register
  - Open blockers from previous week
- Agenda:
  - Review all pilots by lifecycle register state
  - Confirm owner and due date per pilot
  - Verify kickoff scheduling SLA
- Outputs:
  - Updated lifecycle register
  - Prioritized action list for week
  - Escalations assigned

## Wednesday: Blocker Clinic

- Owner: Intake Ops Lead
- Duration: 30 minutes
- Inputs:
  - Blocker register
  - At-risk pilot list
  - Dependency requests
- Agenda:
  - Clear critical blockers
  - Reassign overdue actions
  - Trigger escalation where needed
- Outputs:
  - Resolved blocker log
  - Recovery plans for at-risk pilots
  - Updated due dates and owners

## Friday: KPI Closeout + PLG Sync

- Owner: Founder/PLG Owner
- Duration: 45 minutes
- Inputs:
  - `36-METRICS.md` weekly snapshot
  - Action completion report
  - Wins and frictions summary
- Agenda:
  - Review KPI movement and target status
  - Confirm next-week commitments
  - Prepare build-in-public evidence packet
- Outputs:
  - Weekly scorecard update
  - PLG evidence packet
  - Published/queued LinkedIn update

## Decision Logging

Each meeting records:

- Decision made
- Owner accountable
- Due date
- Validation criterion

## Escalation Rule

Any action overdue by more than 72 hours is escalated in the next cadence touchpoint and cannot roll forward without owner reassignment.

## PLG Content Standard

**Minimum bar:** One LinkedIn post per week, published or queued by Friday EOD.

**Format:** Use the PLG evidence packet schema from `36-RUNBOOK.md` as the content source.
Each post must include at least one concrete data point (metric, milestone, or timeline fact)
from the active pilot register.

**Channel:** Esteban's personal LinkedIn profile.

**Quality floor:** No promotional framing without a concrete client or product evidence point.
Posts may be anonymized (no client name required).

**Failure condition:** If no post ships in a given week, log it as a cadence miss at the following
Wednesday Blocker Clinic. Two consecutive misses trigger a PLG-01 risk flag in the Friday KPI scorecard.
