# Phase 99.1: Future-Grade Quality Evaluation and Governance Closeout - Context

**Gathered:** 2026-04-14
**Status:** Discussed — quality and governance posture locked

<domain>
## Phase Boundary

Add measurable premium-quality gates, ethical guardrails, and non-regression closeout evidence on top of the already-shipped Phase 95, Phase 98, and Phase 99 contracts. This phase is about proving trust, quality, and portability for milestone closeout; it is not a platform rewrite and it does not invent a second reasoning system.

</domain>

<decisions>
## Implementation Decisions

### Quality gate posture
- **D-01:** Premium-quality promotion should use a **per-dimension floor plus an overall verdict**, not an average-only score. Weak dimensions must force review or block rather than being hidden by a strong total.
- **D-02:** The measurable quality gate should explicitly score personalization depth, ICP fit, neuro-fit, naturality, specificity, and grounded usefulness in a machine-readable way.
- **D-03:** The quality gate remains additive to the shipped evaluation envelope and should build on the existing Phase 95 review packaging and the Phase 99 shared tailoring contract.

### Governance and override policy
- **D-04:** Human overrides are allowed, but only with an explicit **audit note, rationale, and evidence references**. No silent bypass path should exist.
- **D-05:** Ethical, evidence-aware, and non-manipulative guardrails remain hard requirements. Unsupported or pressure-heavy neuro language should block or require rewrite rather than pass as premium.

### Non-regression posture
- **D-06:** If preview-safety, provenance, route trace, provider-audit, read-safe, or write-disabled baselines regress, that should **hard-block milestone closeout**.
- **D-07:** v3.6.0 deep-research and governance guarantees remain explicit non-regression baselines and must stay visible in the final closeout evidence.

### Cross-surface visibility
- **D-08:** The final quality verdict should be visible across **all supported surfaces** with the full scorecard, blocking reasons, and required fixes preserved logically across API, MCP, CLI, editor, and internal automation.
- **D-09:** Only presentation formatting may vary by surface; the underlying evaluation and governance semantics must stay identical.

### Scope guardrails
- **D-10:** Phase 99.1 closes the milestone with measurable evaluation and governance evidence only. It must not absorb a new platform layer, separate evaluation service, or alternate reasoning architecture.

### the agent's Discretion
- The exact numeric thresholds for each premium-quality dimension
- The specific blocker and warning codes surfaced in the quality closeout gate
- The final wording and structure of the scorecard and closeout-readiness summary
- The exact evidence bundle shape used for milestone verification and operator review

</decisions>

<specifics>
## Specific Ideas

- Premium output should feel clearly custom, natural, specific, and evidence-backed — not merely polished.
- One weak dimension should be enough to prevent a false “premium-ready” verdict.
- Governance guarantees matter as much as copy quality for the milestone closeout.
- Operators may override, but only transparently and with audit evidence.

</specifics>
