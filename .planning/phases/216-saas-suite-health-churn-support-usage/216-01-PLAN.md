# Plan 216-01 - SaaS Health Score Contract and Explainability

**Phase:** 216 - SaaS Health, Churn, Support, and Product Usage Intelligence  
**Status:** Planned  
**Requirements:** SAS-07, EVD-01..06, QA-01..15

## Objective

Create an explainable health score that operators can trust.

## Scope

- Define SaaSHealthScore with usage, support, billing, engagement, relationship, confidence, trend, and recommended action.
- Store raw contributing facts and weights.
- Link health score outputs to tasks, evidence, and revenue records.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Health score is not black-box.
- Operators can see why a customer is healthy or at risk.
- Low-confidence scores are labeled and task-routed.

## Verification

- Tests cover score calculation, confidence, trend, and explanation fields.
