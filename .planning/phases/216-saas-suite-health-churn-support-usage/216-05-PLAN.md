# Plan 216-05 - Privacy, Retention, and Sensitive Support/Product Data Controls

**Phase:** 216 - SaaS Health, Churn, Support, and Product Usage Intelligence  
**Status:** Planned  
**Requirements:** COMP-01, SAS-07..09, LRN-04, QA-11

## Objective

Protect sensitive support and product usage data.

## Scope

- Classify support text, product usage, account health, billing risk, and customer identifiers.
- Define retention, redaction, export, deletion, and prompt-use rules.
- Ensure learning promotion cannot expose support/product details.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Sensitive support and usage data have retention and redaction controls.
- Prompt and MCP payloads do not expose raw sensitive data unnecessarily.
- Cross-tenant learning receives only anonymized aggregates.

## Verification

- Privacy tests cover PII, ticket text, product event details, and learning handoff.
