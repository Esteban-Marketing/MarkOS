# Plan 216-02 - Product Usage Event Ingest and PLG Signal Map

**Phase:** 216 - SaaS Health, Churn, Support, and Product Usage Intelligence  
**Status:** Planned  
**Requirements:** SAS-07, SAS-09, SG-02, QA-01..15

## Objective

Ingest product usage signals that power health and future PLG without activating growth automation.

## Scope

- Define generic product event ingest with activation, adoption, depth, stickiness, account breadth, expansion, and PQL-compatible fields.
- Map events to health score and future growth signals.
- Preserve privacy, retention, and tenant scoping.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Product usage data feeds health, churn, revenue, and future PLG signals.
- PQL and upgrade-trigger fields remain non-actionable until future GSD contracts exist.
- Event ingest is idempotent and tenant-safe.

## Verification

- Event taxonomy tests cover activation, adoption, stickiness, expansion, and stale-data states.
