# Plan 216-03 - SaaS Support Ticket Intelligence and KB Grounding

**Phase:** 216 - SaaS Health, Churn, Support, and Product Usage Intelligence  
**Status:** Planned  
**Requirements:** SAS-08, EVD-01..06, TASK-04, QA-01..15

## Objective

Turn support tickets into risk, task, and response intelligence with evidence.

## Scope

- Define SaaSSupportTicket with source, SLA, topic, sentiment, urgency, risk, customer, and KB grounding.
- Classify tickets into support, churn, expansion, bug, billing, and product-feedback signals.
- Require CS approval before customer-facing responses unless safe auto-response is explicitly configured.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Suggested responses include KB/evidence grounding.
- Customer-facing support replies are approval-gated by default.
- Support signals feed health and churn tasks.

## Verification

- Tests cover classification, grounding, approval-required response, and redaction.
