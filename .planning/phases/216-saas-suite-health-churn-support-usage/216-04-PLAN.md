# Plan 216-04 - Churn Intervention Playbooks and Tasks

**Phase:** 216 - SaaS Health, Churn, Support, and Product Usage Intelligence  
**Status:** Planned  
**Requirements:** SAS-07, SAS-08, TASK-01..05, QA-01..15

## Objective

Convert churn signals into operator-owned intervention work.

## Scope

- Define playbook templates for billing risk, usage drop, support escalation, champion loss, renewal risk, and expansion opportunity.
- Create tasks with owner, priority, evidence, due date, and recommended action.
- Require approval for save offers, discounts, lifecycle changes, and customer-facing outreach.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Churn risk produces tasks, not passive charts.
- Save offers and discounts require Pricing Engine context and approval.
- Playbooks are evidence-linked and auditable.

## Verification

- Tests cover playbook selection, task creation, approval gates, and suppression.
