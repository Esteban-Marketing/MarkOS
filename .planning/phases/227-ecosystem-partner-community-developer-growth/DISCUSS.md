# Phase 227 - Ecosystem, Partner, Community, and Developer Growth (Discussion)

**Milestone:** v4.2.0 Commercial Engines 1.0  
**Depends on:** Phases 220, 221, 222, 225  
**Quality baseline applies:** all 15 gates

## Goal

Build governed ecosystem distribution across marketplace listings, partners, affiliates, referrals, community, and developer growth.

## Scope

- Partner profiles, certifications, co-sell, and referral/affiliate systems.
- Marketplace listings and developer-growth surfaces.
- Community and champion signal capture.
- Ecosystem attribution and quality controls.
- Shared writeback into CRM, analytics, launches, and learning.

## Non-Goals

- Doc 17 growth-engine foundations remain in phases 218-220.
- Core analytics semantics belong to Phase 225.
- Commercial-stack parity closure belongs to Phase 228.

## Discuss Focus

- Partner quality over partner count.
- Referral and affiliate fraud/risk controls.
- Community and developer signals as operational inputs.
- How marketplace, partner, and community surfaces share one truth.
