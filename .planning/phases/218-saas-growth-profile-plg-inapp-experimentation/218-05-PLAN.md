# Plan 218-05 - MarketingExperiment Registry, ICE Backlog, Guardrails, and Decisions

**Phase:** 218 - SaaS Growth Profile, PLG, In-App, and Experimentation  
**Status:** Planned  
**Requirements:** SG-07, LRN-01..05, QA-01..15

## Objective

Create the experiment system that turns growth ideas into controlled learning.

## Scope

- Define `MarketingExperiment` with hypothesis, surface, variants, sample, metrics, ICE score, guardrails, owner, result, and decision.
- Create an experiment backlog and decision states: launch, reject, extend, redesign, roll out.
- Feed outcomes into ArtifactPerformanceLog, TenantOverlay, and LiteracyUpdateCandidate where appropriate.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Experiments have guardrails before activation.
- Decisions are recorded with evidence.
- Learnings feed the learning/literacy loop.
