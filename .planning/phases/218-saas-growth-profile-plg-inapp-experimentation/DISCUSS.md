# Phase 218 - SaaS Growth Profile, PLG, In-App, and Experimentation (Discussion)

**Milestone:** v4.1.0 SaaS Suite / SaaS Growth Strategy translation  
**Depends on:** Phases 214-217  
**Quality baseline applies:** all 15 gates

## Goal

Translate the first half of `17-SAAS-MARKETING-OS-STRATEGY.md` into governed SaaS growth substrate: SaaS growth mode routing, PLG activation, PQL scoring, upgrade triggers, in-app marketing, and growth experiments.

## Scope

- SaaS growth mode profile: `b2b`, `b2c`, `plg_b2b`, `plg_b2c`, `b2b2c`.
- Activation definitions, milestone funnels, PQL score, and upgrade triggers.
- In-app campaigns with frequency caps, suppression logic, and approval gates.
- Experiment registry with ICE scoring, guardrails, decisions, and learning handoff.

## Non-Goals

- ABM/account expansion and revenue alignment are Phase 219.
- Community/events/PR/partnership/developer marketing are Phase 220.
- No growth agent becomes runnable without AgentRun, approval, pricing, evidence, API/MCP/UI, and tests.
