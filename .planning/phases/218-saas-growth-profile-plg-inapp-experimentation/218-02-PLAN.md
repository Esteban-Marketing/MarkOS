# Plan 218-02 - ActivationDefinition, Milestone Funnels, and PQLScore

**Phase:** 218 - SaaS Growth Profile, PLG, In-App, and Experimentation  
**Status:** Planned  
**Requirements:** SG-02, SAS-09, EVD-01..06, QA-01..15

## Objective

Model activation and product-qualified lead signals from product usage evidence.

## Scope

- Define `ActivationDefinition`, milestone funnel, activation baseline, and PQLScore.
- Link scores to product events, account fit, intent, confidence, and recommended action.
- Keep PQL actions task-only or approval-gated until policy allows more autonomy.

## Acceptance Criteria

- PQL scores are explainable and evidence-linked.
- Low-confidence scores do not trigger customer-facing automation.
- Activation definitions are tenant-configurable and auditable.
