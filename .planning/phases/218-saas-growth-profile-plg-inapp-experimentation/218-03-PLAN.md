# Plan 218-03 - UpgradeTrigger and Pricing-Safe Conversion Prompts

**Phase:** 218 - SaaS Growth Profile, PLG, In-App, and Experimentation  
**Status:** Planned  
**Requirements:** SG-02, SG-11, SG-12, PRC-09, QA-01..15

## Objective

Create upgrade triggers that respect pricing, approval, and customer-experience controls.

## Scope

- Define `UpgradeTrigger` with product event, usage threshold, segment, timing, channel, Pricing Engine context, and suppression rules.
- Require PricingRecommendation or placeholder for pricing-sensitive prompts.
- Route conversion prompts through approval unless earned autonomy is explicitly configured.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Upgrade prompts cannot invent prices, discounts, or packages.
- Triggered actions are frequency-capped and auditable.
- Human approvals are required by default for customer-facing nudges.
