# Plan 218-04 - InAppCampaign Orchestration, Suppression, and Approvals

**Phase:** 218 - SaaS Growth Profile, PLG, In-App, and Experimentation  
**Status:** Planned  
**Requirements:** SG-05, SG-09, SG-12, QA-01..15

## Objective

Define governed in-app marketing campaigns.

## Scope

- Define `InAppCampaign` with segment, trigger, format, frequency cap, suppression, goal, success event, and results.
- Coordinate with email, support, CS, and product events.
- Require approval for customer-facing experiences by default.

## Acceptance Criteria

- In-app campaigns have suppression logic and customer-experience guardrails.
- Campaigns create tasks, approvals, experiments, or learnings.
- No prompt conflicts with support/CS state or billing/legal risk.
