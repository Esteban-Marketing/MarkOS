# Plan 204-13 - v2 Compliance Guardrails for CLI GA

**Phase:** 204 - CLI `markos` v1 GA  
**Status:** Planned  
**Type:** Gap-closure plan added after the 2026-04-22 v2 vault/codebase compliance audit

## Objective

Make the CLI GA surface compatible with the v2 Marketing Operating System doctrine before release. The CLI should not ship as a generic command wrapper; it must understand AgentRun visibility, approval states, connector failures, cost/budget posture, Pricing Engine placeholder rules, and newly ingested vault modules such as the SaaS Suite and SaaS Marketing OS Strategy.

## Scope

- Extend `markos run`, `markos status`, and `markos doctor` acceptance criteria to include v2 operating-loop checks.
- Ensure `markos status` surfaces recent AgentRuns, approval blockers, budget/quota state, connector recovery issues, and Pricing Engine placeholder status when pricing is unresolved.
- Ensure `markos doctor --check-only` can fail CI when the vault, PageIndex, contracts, or Pricing Engine placeholder policy are stale.
- Ensure `markos doctor --check-only` treats new incoming docs and PageIndex drift, including `16-SAAS-SUITE.md` and `17-SAAS-MARKETING-OS-STRATEGY.md`, as freshness issues.
- Ensure CLI docs do not publish hard-coded MarkOS price points.
- Route winget and apt distribution out of Phase 205 and into a future distribution-hardening phase or 204.x gap closure.

## Out of Scope

- Implementing Pricing Engine schema or MCP tools. That belongs to Phase 205.
- Implementing SaaS Suite schema, API, MCP, or UI. That belongs to Phases 214-217.
- Implementing SaaS Marketing OS growth modules. That belongs to a future post-217 GSD translation.
- Implementing AgentRun v2 DAG substrate. That belongs to Phase 207.
- Building the human operating UI. That belongs to Phase 208.

## Acceptance Criteria

- `markos doctor --check-only` includes vault/PageIndex freshness, contracts/OpenAPI freshness, `.markos-local/` safety, and pricing-placeholder checks.
- `markos status` has a documented output section for AgentRun health, approvals, connector recovery, and billing/pricing state.
- CLI docs explain `{{MARKOS_PRICING_ENGINE_PENDING}}` and forbid public price hardcoding.
- CLI docs mention that SaaS Suite pricing, discounts, save offers, and packages remain Pricing Engine-owned until approved recommendations exist.
- Phase 204 context no longer routes winget/apt work into Phase 205.
- Tests cover at least one pricing-placeholder policy failure and one AgentRun/status shape.

## Dependencies

- Phase 204 plans 204-06, 204-08, 204-09, and 204-12.
- Phase 205 doctrine for Pricing Engine placeholder behavior.
- SaaS Suite doctrine for `business_type=saas` tenant-suite freshness checks.
- SaaS Marketing OS Strategy canon for future growth-module freshness checks.
- Phase 207 final AgentRun v2 schema may require a later CLI compatibility follow-up.
