<threat_model>
- T-95-01 (false promotion): weakly grounded output could score too highly and slip into a promotable state. Mitigation: make grounding and citation integrity the dominant weighted dimension and allow hard blockers to override total score.
- T-95-02 (opaque ranking): operators could see a winner without understanding why it won. Mitigation: require winner/runner-up explanations and dimension-level score breakdowns.
- T-95-03 (silent pass state): the contract could imply acceptance or apply semantics instead of evaluation only. Mitigation: keep the envelope read-safe and separate promotable from accepted.
- T-95-04 (diagnostic drift): blockers and warnings could vary by surface and lose machine readability. Mitigation: define one stable reason-code contract from the start.
</threat_model>
