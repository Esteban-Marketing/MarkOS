# Phase 222 - CRM Timeline and Commercial Memory Workspace (Discussion)

**Milestone:** v4.2.0 Commercial Engines 1.0  
**Depends on:** Phases 208, 209, 211, 221  
**Quality baseline applies:** all 15 gates

## Goal

Evolve MarkOS into a timeline-first CRM with customer 360, account/person/opportunity memory, lifecycle orchestration, and next-best-action workspaces.

## Scope

- Customer 360 objects for account, person, opportunity, lifecycle, and health.
- Timeline-first event model shared across marketing, sales, success, support, and billing.
- Commercial memory, ownership, and next-best-action surfaces.
- Buying committee, expansion, renewal, and risk context.
- CRM writeback into tasks, approvals, pricing, analytics, and launches.

## Non-Goals

- Identity and audience substrate belongs to Phase 221.
- Email and messaging delivery belongs to Phase 223.
- Sales enablement proof packs and battlecards belong to Phase 226.

## Discuss Focus

- Customer 360 object model and relationship boundaries.
- Timeline event taxonomy and source precedence.
- Shared memory across marketing, sales, CS, support, and finance.
- How current CRM work should be reused vs replaced.

## Proposed Plan Slices

| Slice | Purpose |
|---|---|
| 222-01 | Customer360, AccountWorkspace, and PersonWorkspace contracts |
| 222-02 | Timeline event taxonomy, provenance, and writeback rules |
| 222-03 | Opportunity, lifecycle, risk, and renewal object model |
| 222-04 | Next-best-action, task routing, and ownership semantics |
| 222-05 | CRM UI/API/MCP workspace posture |
| 222-06 | Migration, import, and testing strategy for commercial memory |
