**# Ecommerce Tone and Naturality Guidance**
- Opening (2 sentences): Note overlay doc provides core posture. This doc adds funnel-stage specifics for transaction mechanics: cart acquisition, checkout conversion, abandon recovery, and repeat purchase.
- Note distinction from B2C: ecommerce = transaction mechanics; B2C = brand identity + relationship (broader).

**## EVIDENCE BASE** (2 short bullets):
- Baymard Institute: average documented cart abandonment rate is 70.19% across ecommerce — cart abandon recovery email has 45% open rate.
- Post-purchase email at day 7 drives 2-5x higher review submission rate than generic follow-up.

**## CORE TACTICS** per stage:
- **Awareness**: Product discovery. Meta/Google Shopping visibility. Feed-quality imagery, benefit-first caption, scroll-stopping hook. Channel: Meta, Google Shopping, TikTok.
- **Consideration**: Reassurance. Remove comparison anxiety: detailed product photos, sizing/specs, return policy prominent. Reviews and Q&A visible. No friction between "I want this" and "I'll buy it".
- **Decision**: Urgency credible. Use real stock counts ("3 left"), real-deadline shipping windows ("order by 3pm for same-day"). Review excerpts at checkout layer. Trust badges (secure checkout, easy returns).
- **Onboarding**: Purchase confirmation. Order summary clear, shipping ETA specific, next steps (returns process, review ask timing). Reduce post-purchase anxiety.
- **Retention**: Repurchase as natural next step. Use purchase patterns to time re-order prompts. Complement upsell ("others who bought X also got Y"). Loyalty reward milestone.

**## COUNTER-INDICATORS**:
- Manufactured scarcity ("Only 2 left!" when there are hundreds in inventory).
- Excessive urgency at Consideration before the buyer has seen enough product proof.
- Generic post-purchase drip email with no product-specific follow-up.

```markdown
# Ecommerce Starter Pack

**Business model:** Online retail — selling physical or digital products through an online store, where conversion is measured by cart completion and LTV is built through repeat purchase.
**Primary marketing challenges:**
- Cart abandonment: 70%+ of carts are abandoned before checkout
- Product discovery: getting products in front of buyers at the right scroll moment
- Repeat purchase: converting one-time buyers to subscribed/loyal customers

**Disciplines covered:** Paid_Media · Content_SEO · Lifecycle_Email · Social · Landing_Pages

## When to use this pack
Use for DTC brands, marketplace sellers, online retail stores, and subscription box products. Signals: SKU-based product catalog, checkout flow on site, purchase conversion is the primary goal metric.

## Pack contents
| File | Description |
|------|-------------|
| Paid_Media/PROMPTS.md | Starter prompts for Meta Shopping, Google Shopping, and DTC retargeting |
| Content_SEO/PROMPTS.md | Starter prompts for buying guides, category pages, and product SEO |
| Lifecycle_Email/PROMPTS.md | Starter prompts for cart recover, post-purchase, and repurchase sequences |
| Social/PROMPTS.md | Starter prompts for product UGC, reviews, and purchase-trigger content |
| Landing_Pages/PROMPTS.md | Starter prompts for product pages, collection pages, and promo landing pages |
```

**Paid_Media/PROMPTS.md** (Ecommerce — Paid Media):
> Context: Ecommerce paid media is performance-heavy — Meta Shopping/DPA, Google Shopping, and dynamic retargeting account for the majority of ROAS. Prompts reflect product-level ad copy, not brand awareness.

Write 4 prompts: (1) Meta Dynamic Product Ad (DPA) creative brief — primary text and headline for a carousel retargeting product viewers who didn't add to cart, (2) Google Shopping product title optimization brief for a competitive category (title structure: brand + product type + key attribute), (3) TikTok product ad brief showing product in use, testimonial hook, CTA to product page (UGC style), (4) Win-back retargeting ad for lapsed customers (last purchase 90+ days ago) with re-engagement offer.

**Content_SEO/PROMPTS.md** (Ecommerce — Content & SEO):
> Context: Ecommerce SEO wins on product category pages, buying guides, and comparison content. Long-tail product queries convert 3-5x better than generic informational. Content supports both organic discovery and internal linking.

Write 4 prompts: (1) "Best [product type] for [use case/budget/person]" buying guide outline targeting a commercial investigation keyword, (2) Category page copy brief (above-fold description, benefit bullets, buying guidance, internal link structure), (3) Product description template for a hero SKU (hook, 3 benefit bullets, materials/specs, social proof, FAQ), (4) Seasonal/promo content brief targeting a gift-buying keyword with purchase urgency.

**Lifecycle_Email/PROMPTS.md** (Ecommerce — Lifecycle Email):
> Context: Ecommerce email is behavior-triggered and purchase-timed. The three highest-ROI sequences are: cart abandonment (send within 1hr), post-purchase (day 7), and repurchase prompt (based on reorder window for consumable products).

Write 4 prompts: (1) Cart abandonment 3-email sequence: Email 1 (1hr, show cart + low-pressure reminder), Email 2 (24hr, add social proof or review), Email 3 (72hr, add incentive if appropriate), (2) Post-purchase Day 7 email (satisfaction check-in + review request + product care tip or complement), (3) Repurchase prompt for a consumable product (timing based on average reorder cycle, urgency framed as "running low?" not a promotion), (4) Browse abandonment email for high-AOV product viewer who didn't add to cart (show alternatives + reviews + inventory note).

**Social/PROMPTS.md** (Ecommerce — Social Media):
> Context: Ecommerce social converts through product-first visual content: real reviews, product in use, and UGC that removes purchase hesitation. TikTok drives discovery; Instagram drives aspiration; Facebook retargeting drives conversion.

Write 4 prompts: (1) UGC-style Instagram Reel brief — customer showing product in context of their daily life (day-in-the-life integration, authentic, non-scripted feel), (2) Review/testimonial social post — pull a specific customer quote with product + outcome, add product image, structured hook, (3) "Product drop" announcement post for a new SKU launch (exclusivity framing, benefit-first, link to product page), (4) Before/after or problem-solution product demo (short-form video brief for TikTok/Reels, show the gap the product closes).

**Landing_Pages/PROMPTS.md** (Ecommerce — Landing Pages):
> Context: Ecommerce landing pages must reduce purchase friction. Product pages need trust signals, reviews, and a clear path to checkout. Promo pages need urgency cues that are credible (real deadlines, real stock).

Write 4 prompts: (1) Hero product page copy brief (above-fold: benefit-led headline + product image + reviews count + CTA, below-fold: features table + size guide + returns policy + Q&A + review excerpts), (2) New customer "first order" promotional landing page (welcome offer, top products grid, trust signals, countdown if real deadline), (3) Bundle/upsell landing page brief (show the value of 2-3 products together vs. separate, bundled price clarity, "frequently bought together" social proof), (4) Promo event landing page (Black Friday, seasonal sale) with countdown, featured categories, credibility statement about discount authenticity.
