# Plan 208-02 - Morning Brief Surface

**Phase:** 208 - Human Operating Interface  
**Status:** Planned  
**Requirements:** TASK-02, RUN-05, CONN-04, LOOP-07, QA-01..15

## Objective

Create the daily decision surface for urgent work, risk, opportunities, and wins.

## Scope

- Aggregate approvals, run failures, connector issues, budget risk, pricing warnings, top opportunities, and recent wins.
- Link every brief item to a task, approval, run, evidence, connector, or revenue record.
- Define priority ordering and owner display.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Every Morning Brief item has owner, reason, urgency, and next action.
- Budget/pricing/connector risks are visible without hunting through settings.
- Brief content is tenant-scoped and evidence-linked.

## Verification

- Data-contract tests cover each brief section and empty states.
