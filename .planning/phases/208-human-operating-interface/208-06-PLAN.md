# Plan 208-06 - Weekly Narrative

**Phase:** 208 - Human Operating Interface  
**Status:** Planned  
**Requirements:** LOOP-07, LOOP-08, LRN-02, QA-01..15

## Objective

Produce a weekly operator narrative that explains outcomes and next actions.

## Scope

- Tie completed work, approvals, dispatches, outcomes, connector health, budget/cost, and revenue or leading indicators into a short narrative.
- Link narrative claims to EvidenceMap and performance logs.
- Generate next tasks or strategy recommendations when outcomes change.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Weekly Narrative cites source records instead of unsupported prose.
- Outcomes connect to pipeline, leading indicators, or explicit measurement gaps.
- Next actions become tasks, not passive suggestions.

## Verification

- Narrative tests assert evidence links, no unsupported claims, and task creation from recommendations.
