# Plan 208-04 - Approval Inbox

**Phase:** 208 - Human Operating Interface  
**Status:** Planned  
**Requirements:** TASK-04, EVD-05, RUN-06, PRC-09, QA-01..15

## Objective

Create a single approval surface for customer-facing and external-world mutations.

## Scope

- Support preview, evidence, brand score, compliance risk, cost, Pricing Engine context, edit, reject, approve, and rejection reason.
- Cover content publish, social reply/post, email/sequence, ad/CRM mutation, billing correction, support reply, connector action, and price/package change.
- Record decision lineage back to AgentRun and tasks.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Unsupported claims and pricing-sensitive gaps block approval.
- Approvals show enough evidence for a human to decide quickly.
- Rejection reasons return to the originating run/task.

## Verification

- Approval UI tests cover approve/edit/reject and blocked evidence states.
