# Plan 214-04 - SaaS Customer Identity Bridge

**Phase:** 214 - SaaS Suite Activation and Subscription Core  
**Status:** Planned  
**Requirements:** SAS-03, LOOP-06, QA-01..15

## Objective

Link SaaS subscriptions to CRM/customer/account identity without creating a disconnected customer model.

## Scope

- Map subscription owners to CRM contacts, companies, accounts, and deals where possible.
- Define bridge records for processor customer IDs, CRM IDs, and SaaS subscription IDs.
- Define conflict and merge review behavior.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Subscription data can connect to CRM and revenue intelligence.
- Identity conflicts create review tasks.
- Tenant isolation and deletion/export behavior are preserved.

## Verification

- Identity bridge tests cover single match, multiple matches, missing CRM, and merge-review states.
