# Plan 214-01 - SaaS Activation Gate, Wizard, and ID Allocation

**Phase:** 214 - SaaS Suite Activation and Subscription Core  
**Status:** Planned  
**Requirements:** SAS-01, SAS-02, PRC-09, QA-01..15

## Objective

Define how SaaS Suite becomes available only for eligible SaaS tenants.

## Scope

- Reserve fresh contract and migration IDs for SaaS Suite activation.
- Define activation wizard requirements for business type, countries, processors, accounting, legal billing, enabled modules, and autonomy posture.
- Gate SaaS Suite routes and APIs behind `business_type = saas` and explicit operator activation.
- Preserve Pricing Engine placeholder behavior for unresolved plan/pricing fields.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Non-SaaS tenants cannot access SaaS Suite surfaces.
- Activation records country, processor, accounting, legal billing, module, and approval posture.
- Pricing-sensitive fields defer to Pricing Engine when unresolved.

## Verification

- Activation-gating tests cover SaaS, non-SaaS, inactive, and partially configured tenants.
