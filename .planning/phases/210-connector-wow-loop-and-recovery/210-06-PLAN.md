# Plan 210-06 - Backfill, Retry, and Recovery Evidence

**Phase:** 210 - Connector Wow Loop and Recovery  
**Status:** Planned  
**Requirements:** CONN-01, CONN-04, EVD-03, COMP-01, QA-01..15

## Objective

Make connector sync and backfill replay-safe, observable, and auditable.

## Scope

- Define cursor, sync window, retry, DLQ, backfill, and retention behavior.
- Link sync attempts to evidence and recovery tasks.
- Record partial sync and data freshness status.
- Preserve privacy and deletion/export controls.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Backfill is idempotent and cursor-based.
- Partial or failed sync is visible and does not masquerade as complete data.
- Recovery evidence is available for SOC2 and support.

## Verification

- Tests cover duplicate sync, partial failure, retry, DLQ, and retention expiry.
