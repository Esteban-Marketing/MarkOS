# Plan 209-04 - Approval UI Evidence Exposure and Claim Blocking

**Phase:** 209 - Evidence, Research, and Claim Safety  
**Status:** Planned  
**Requirements:** EVD-02, EVD-05, TASK-04, QA-01..15

## Objective

Expose evidence and risk inside approvals so unsupported claims cannot slip through.

## Scope

- Add evidence summaries, source quality, TTL, assumptions, unsupported claims, and edit suggestions to approval payloads.
- Block external dispatch for unsupported required claims.
- Allow humans to edit claims, request research, reject, or approve only when policy permits.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Approval UI shows why a claim is safe, risky, stale, or unsupported.
- Unsupported claims block publish/send/social/pricing/support actions by default.
- Every override has reason, actor, and audit lineage.

## Verification

- UI and contract tests cover blocked, editable, and approved claim states.
