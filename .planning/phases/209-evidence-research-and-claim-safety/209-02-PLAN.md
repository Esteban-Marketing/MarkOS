# Plan 209-02 - Source Quality Score and Research Tier Policy

**Phase:** 209 - Evidence, Research, and Claim Safety  
**Status:** Planned  
**Requirements:** EVD-01, EVD-03, EVD-06, QA-01..15

## Objective

Define how MarkOS scores source trust and research depth.

## Scope

- Implement the Source Quality Score rubric from the v2 spec.
- Define research tiers and minimum source requirements by claim class.
- Map first-party, official, analyst, public web, social/community, and operator-provided sources.
- Define downgrade behavior for weak, stale, or contradictory sources.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Claim approval can reason about source class and quality.
- Pricing, competitor, legal, compliance, and security claims require stronger evidence.
- Low-quality evidence is labeled or blocked.

## Verification

- Unit tests cover source scoring, downgrade rules, and claim-class thresholds.
