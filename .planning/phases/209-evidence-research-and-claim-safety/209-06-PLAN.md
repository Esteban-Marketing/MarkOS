**Phase:** 209 - Evidence, Research, and Claim Safety  
**Status:** Planned  
**Requirements:** EVD-01..06, QA-08, QA-15

## Objective

Lock evidence behavior with automated tests and eval cases.

## Scope

- Add deterministic test fixtures for supported, unsupported, low-quality, stale, inferred, and contradictory claims.
- Add eval cases for hallucination defense and citation quality.
- Cover pricing, competitor, content, social, SaaS, and public proof examples.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Inference labels are explicit.
- Pricing and compliance examples require stronger evidence.
