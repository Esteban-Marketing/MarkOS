# Plan 209-03 - Claim TTL, Freshness, Stale Context, and Known Gaps

**Phase:** 209 - Evidence, Research, and Claim Safety  
**Status:** Planned  
**Requirements:** EVD-01, EVD-02, EVD-04, QA-01..15

## Objective

Prevent stale evidence from silently supporting fresh claims.

## Scope

- Define TTL defaults for pricing, competitor, legal, product, revenue, compliance, brand, and evergreen literacy claims.
- Add freshness checks and known-gap fields.
- Define stale-context behavior for agents and approval UI.
- Define re-research triggers.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Expired volatile claims cannot approve external dispatch.
- Known gaps are visible to agents and humans.
- Agents reuse non-stale research before starting new research.

## Verification

- Tests cover fresh, stale, expired, contradictory, and known-gap evidence.
