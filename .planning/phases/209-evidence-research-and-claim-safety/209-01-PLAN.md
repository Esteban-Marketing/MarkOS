# Plan 209-01 - EvidenceMap Contracts, Schema, and ID Allocation

**Phase:** 209 - Evidence, Research, and Claim Safety  
**Status:** Planned  
**Requirements:** EVD-01..06, QA-01..15

## Objective

Make factual evidence a central object instead of domain-specific metadata.

## Scope

- Reserve fresh contract and migration IDs.
- Define EvidenceMap, EvidenceSource, Claim, ResearchContext, and EvidenceDecision references.
- Include source URL, source class, source quality score, freshness, confidence, TTL, known gaps, and inference label.
- Add tenant scoping, RLS, redaction, and audit posture.

## Acceptance Criteria

- EvidenceMap can support content, pricing, social, sales, support, SaaS, and public claims.
- Every factual customer-facing claim can link to evidence or be marked unsupported.
- Evidence contracts do not leak raw tenant secrets.

## Verification

- Contract tests cover supported, unsupported, expired, inferred, and redacted evidence.
