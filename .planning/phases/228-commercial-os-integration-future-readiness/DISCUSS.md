# Phase 228 - Commercial OS Integration and Future-Readiness (Discussion)

**Milestone:** v4.2.0 Commercial Engines 1.0  
**Depends on:** Phases 221-227  
**Quality baseline applies:** all 15 gates

## Goal

Close the commercial-engine lane by enforcing shared contracts, UI/API/MCP parity, migration posture, provider replaceability, testing obligations, and no-obsolescence guarantees across the full stack.

## Scope

- Cross-engine parity gates and contract alignment.
- Provider adapter and export/migration posture.
- Commercial-stack testing doctrine enforcement.
- Future-ready quality and anti-obsolescence rules.
- Final integration audit before execution broadens further.

## Non-Goals

- This phase does not replace the earlier engine phases.
- It does not reopen Pricing Engine or SaaS Suite foundations.
- It does not invent new commercial domains beyond docs 18-26.

## Discuss Focus

- What "future-ready" means in enforceable engineering terms.
- Migration/export and provider-replacement guarantees.
- UI/API/MCP parity requirements.
- How to prevent new silos or hidden side-effect paths.
