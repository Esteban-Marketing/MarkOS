# Plan 220-05 - Partnerships, Affiliate, and Developer Marketing Workflows

**Phase:** 220 - SaaS Community, Events, PR, Partnerships, Developer Marketing, and Growth Agent Surface  
**Status:** Planned  
**Requirements:** SG-06, SG-11, SG-12, QA-01..15

## Objective

Model partner, affiliate, and developer marketing without bypassing compliance.

## Scope

- Define partner and affiliate program records with approval, payout, prohibited methods, tracking, and performance.
- Define developer marketing objects for docs-as-product, API examples, changelog discipline, starter kits, and developer community.
- Keep payouts behind Phase 215 compliance hooks.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Partner and affiliate incentives are approval, fraud, tax, and billing aware.
- Developer marketing creates evidence-backed tasks and artifacts.
- No payout or public partner claim bypasses controls.
