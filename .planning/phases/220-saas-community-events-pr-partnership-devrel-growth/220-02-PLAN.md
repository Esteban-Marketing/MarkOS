# Plan 220-02 - CommunityProfile and Community Health Workflows

**Phase:** 220 - SaaS Community, Events, PR, Partnerships, Developer Marketing, and Growth Agent Surface  
**Status:** Planned  
**Requirements:** SG-06, SG-09, QA-01..15

## Objective

Model community-led growth as a governed workflow.

## Scope

- Define `CommunityProfile` with platform, goals, member count, activity, health score, launch status, and URL.
- Route community signals to tasks, content ideas, support/product feedback, and learning.
- Require approval for external community posts or moderation actions.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Community data creates tasks, recommendations, or learning.
- Public/community actions are approval-aware.
- Community health can feed SaaS health and advocacy.
