# Plan 220-04 - PR, Analyst, and Review Intelligence

**Phase:** 220 - SaaS Community, Events, PR, Partnerships, Developer Marketing, and Growth Agent Surface  
**Status:** Planned  
**Requirements:** SG-06, EVD-01..06, QA-01..15

## Objective

Govern press, analyst, public review, and marketplace proof workflows.

## Scope

- Define journalist/analyst/review target records, pitch context, outreach approval, coverage tracking, and evidence.
- Link G2/Capterra review requests and pricing profile sync to approval and Pricing Engine policy.
- Block unsupported claims in public proof.

## Acceptance Criteria

- PR/review outreach is evidence-backed and approval-gated.
- Public claims are sourced and freshness-aware.
- Pricing profile changes cannot bypass Pricing Engine.
