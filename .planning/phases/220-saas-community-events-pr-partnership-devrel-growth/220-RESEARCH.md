# Phase 220 Research - SaaS Community, Events, PR, Partnerships, Developer Marketing, and Growth Agent Surface

## Primary research question

How should MarkOS govern the broad SaaS growth engines that touch external audiences, incentives, communities, events, press, partners, developers, and public proof?

## Current-code support

- Phase 203 webhook guarantees, Phase 210 connector substrate, Phase 209 evidence, Phase 208 approvals/tasks, and Phase 205 pricing controls provide the safety base.
- SaaS Suite phases 214-217 provide customer, billing, support, usage, revenue, and agent surfaces.

## Gaps to solve

- No ReferralProgram, ViralLoopMetrics, CommunityProfile, MarketingEvent, AffiliateProgram, partnership, PR/review, analyst, or developer marketing object exists.
- No external mutation or payout control matrix exists for these growth engines.
- No growth API/MCP/UI closure exists.

## Tests implied

- Referral fraud/incentive guardrail tests.
- Event, PR, review, and partner approval tests.
- Public claim evidence tests.
- Growth target agent non-runnable and readiness tests.
