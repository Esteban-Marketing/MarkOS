# Plan 220-01 - ReferralProgram and ViralLoopMetrics

**Phase:** 220 - SaaS Community, Events, PR, Partnerships, Developer Marketing, and Growth Agent Surface  
**Status:** Planned  
**Requirements:** SG-04, SG-11, SG-12, QA-01..15

## Objective

Model viral and referral growth with incentive and fraud controls.

## Scope

- Define `ReferralProgram` and `ViralLoopMetrics`.
- Include incentives, attribution window, share tools, fraud controls, payout hooks, and performance.
- Route rewards and commissions through pricing/billing compliance and approval.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Referral rewards cannot bypass Pricing Engine, billing, tax, fraud, approval, or audit controls.
- Viral metrics are evidence-linked.
- Customer-facing referral prompts are approval-gated by default.
