# Plan 219-05 - Expansion, Save, Discount, and Pricing Engine Controls

**Phase:** 219 - SaaS B2B Expansion, ABM, and Revenue Alignment  
**Status:** Planned  
**Requirements:** SG-11, SG-12, PRC-01..09, QA-01..15

## Objective

Ensure expansion and save motions do not bypass Pricing Engine or approvals.

## Scope

- Route discounts, save offers, expansion packages, annual prompts, and pricing copy through PricingRecommendation or placeholder.
- Require approval for customer-facing pricing and package changes.
- Link offers to revenue, health, and evidence records.

## Acceptance Criteria

- No discount/save/expansion price is generated without Pricing Engine context.
- Customer-facing offers require approval.
- Offer outcomes feed learning.
