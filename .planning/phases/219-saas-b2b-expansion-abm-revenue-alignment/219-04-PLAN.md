# Plan 219-04 - Customer Advocacy, Review Request, and Proof Workflows

**Phase:** 219 - SaaS B2B Expansion, ABM, and Revenue Alignment  
**Status:** Planned  
**Requirements:** SG-03, SG-06, T0-04, QA-01..15

## Objective

Turn customer success into governed advocacy and proof workflows.

## Scope

- Define advocacy candidates, review requests, case-study readiness, testimonial proof, consent, and privacy posture.
- Link public claims to EvidenceMap and approval.
- Prevent unsupported G2/Capterra or review-generation claims.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Review and proof workflows require evidence, consent, and approval.
- Public proof is privacy-safe.
- Planned claims are clearly separated from shipped evidence.
