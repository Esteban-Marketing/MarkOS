# Plan 219-06 - B2B Growth Agent Readiness and Non-Runnable Gates

**Phase:** 219 - SaaS B2B Expansion, ABM, and Revenue Alignment  
**Status:** Planned  
**Requirements:** SG-10, RUN-01..08, QA-01..15

## Objective

Define readiness gates for ABM, expansion, advocacy, and revenue alignment agents.

## Scope

- Register target ABM/REV/customer marketing agents as planned until contracts and controls exist.
- Define inputs, outputs, costs, approvals, evidence, and failure behavior.
- Block external actions without explicit readiness and approval posture.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Target B2B growth agents remain non-runnable until ready.
- Agent readiness matrix links to API/MCP/UI and tests.
- Failures create tasks.
