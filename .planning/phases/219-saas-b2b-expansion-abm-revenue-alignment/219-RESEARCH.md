# Phase 219 Research - SaaS B2B Expansion, ABM, and Revenue Alignment

## Primary research question

How should MarkOS turn SaaS account, customer, usage, support, revenue, and CRM facts into governed B2B expansion, ABM, customer marketing, advocacy, and revenue alignment workflows?

## Current-code support

- CRM entities, reporting, attribution, outbound, and copilot foundations exist.
- SaaS Suite phases 214-217 plan subscription, health, support, usage, and revenue facts.
- Evidence, approval, task, and Pricing Engine phases provide safety controls.

## Gaps to solve

- No `RevenueTeamConfig`, `CustomerMarketingProgram`, `ABMAccountPackage`, or buying committee model exists.
- No shared MQL/SQL/PQL/SLA feedback loop exists.
- No account expansion playbook or advocacy proof workflow exists.

## Tests implied

- Revenue lifecycle config tests.
- ABM package evidence and approval tests.
- Expansion offer Pricing Engine and approval tests.
- Advocacy/review proof and consent tests.
