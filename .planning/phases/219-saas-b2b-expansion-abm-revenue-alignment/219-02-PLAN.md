# Plan 219-02 - Account Expansion and Customer Marketing Programs

**Phase:** 219 - SaaS B2B Expansion, ABM, and Revenue Alignment  
**Status:** Planned  
**Requirements:** SG-03, SG-09, QA-01..15

## Objective

Model expansion and customer marketing as governed programs.

## Scope

- Define `CustomerMarketingProgram` for expansion, advocacy, retention, education, beta, advisory, and co-marketing.
- Link eligible accounts to health, usage, support, revenue, and CRM evidence.
- Create tasks and approvals for outreach or offers.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Programs create tasks, approvals, experiments, or learnings.
- Eligibility is evidence-linked.
- Customer-facing actions require approval by default.
