# Plan 219-03 - ABMAccountPackage and Buying Committee Mapping

**Phase:** 219 - SaaS B2B Expansion, ABM, and Revenue Alignment  
**Status:** Planned  
**Requirements:** SG-03, EVD-01..06, QA-01..15

## Objective

Create ABM account intelligence packages with evidence and approval posture.

## Scope

- Define `ABMAccountPackage` with account tier, company profile, buying committee, strategic signals, messaging, engagement, and recommended actions.
- Link claims and recommendations to EvidenceMap.
- Route account-personalized content through Approval Inbox.

## Acceptance Criteria

- ABM recommendations are sourced and freshness-aware.
- Buying committee assumptions are labeled when inferred.
- Account-personalized external actions require approval.
