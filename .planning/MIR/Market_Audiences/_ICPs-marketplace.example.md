# ICPs.md — Ideal Customer Profiles (Dual Side)
# Reference Example: **Marketplace** (Freelance Creative Services Platform)

<!-- markos-token: MIR | model: Marketplace -->
> [!NOTE] Marketplace ICP requires separate profiles for supply side (sellers) and demand side (buyers). Both sides must reach critical mass for the platform to function.

---

## 1. Supply Side ICP — Freelance Creative (Seller)

### Overview
The ideal supply-side participant is an **independent creative professional** with 3+ years of experience, an established portfolio, and an intent to grow their income through inbound client acquisition rather than cold outreach. They are not hobbyists — they have real deliverables and real clients.

### Supply Side Attributes

| Attribute | Ideal Value | Acceptable Range |
|-----------|-------------|------------------|
| Creative Discipline | Graphic design, brand identity, motion design | Any visual or digital creative discipline |
| Years of Experience | 3 – 10 years | 2+ years with portfolio |
| Annual Freelance Revenue | $40K – $150K | $20K+ (part-time or full-time freelance) |
| Portfolio Quality | Professional, niche-specific, self-directed | Demonstrates client work + original projects |
| Platform Familiarity | Has used Upwork, Fiverr (and is frustrated by price competition) | Any freelance platform experience |
| Response Time | <4 hours during business hours | <24 hours |
| Geography | US, UK, Canada, Western Europe | English-proficiency required |

### Supply Side Disqualifiers
* Beginners with under-developed portfolios — lowers platform quality perception.
* Creatives who only take project types they've done before — reduces breadth.
* Unavailable >2 weeks per month — reliability is a core platform KPI.

---

## 2. Demand Side ICP — Business Hiring Creative (Buyer)

### Overview
The ideal buyer is a **marketing-led company** that has recurring creative needs but has not yet hired an in-house designer. They understand the value of good design and are not primarily price-shopping — they are looking for reliability and capability.

### Demand Side Attributes

| Attribute | Ideal Value | Acceptable Range |
|-----------|-------------|------------------|
| Company Size | 10 – 250 employees | 5 – 1,000 employees |
| Marketing Function | Has at least 1 dedicated marketing person | Any team with external-facing brand needs |
| Design Budget | $2K – $20K/year on freelance creative | $500+ per project |
| Hiring Frequency | 2+ projects per quarter | 1+ project per year (upgrade path) |
| Past Freelance Experience | Has used Upwork/Fiverr and had mixed results (quality inconsistency) | First-time platform user |
| Brand Maturity | Has a logo and basic brand identity | Any stage — brand creation is a service |

### Demand Side Disqualifiers
* Companies that only need one-off logo design and won't return — low LTV, high onboarding cost.
* Enterprise procurement-heavy companies requiring extensive vendor registration before first purchase.
* Companies expecting unlimited revisions at fixed price — poor fit for the platform model.
