## 1. Functional Jobs
*The practical tasks the customer is trying to complete (e.g., "Automate my reporting").*

## 2. Emotional Jobs
*How the customer wants to feel (e.g., "Secure", "Powerful", "In Control").*

## 3. Social Jobs
*How the customer wants to be perceived by others (e.g., "The innovator in the team").*

## 4. Pains
*Specific frustrations, risks, and obstacles the customer is avoiding.*

## 5. Gains
*Positive outcomes, surprises, and concrete benefits the customer hopes for.*
