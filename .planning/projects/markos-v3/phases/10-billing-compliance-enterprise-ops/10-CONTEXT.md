# Phase 10 Context: Billing, Compliance, Enterprise Operations

## Goal

Operationalize monetization, governance controls, and enterprise readiness for production adoption.

## Requirements

BIL-01, BIL-02, BIL-03, BIL-04, SEC-01, SEC-02, SEC-03, OPS-01, OPS-02, IAM-04

## In scope

- subscription and entitlement controls
- metering and invoice reconciliation
- security and privacy operational controls
- SLO dashboards, alerting, and incident runbooks
- enterprise readiness evidence mapping

## Out of scope

- post-v3 autonomous modes and expanded CRM scope

## Must be true

1. Metering aligns with invoices and entitlements.
2. Security and privacy workflows are testable and documented.
3. Operational dashboards and incident playbooks are production-usable.
