# MILESTONE-CONTEXT.md

## Proposed Milestone

**Version:** v3.7.0  
**Working Name:** Neuro-Adaptive Literacy Intelligence  
**Status:** Discussion draft ready for milestone initialization  
**Captured:** 2026-04-14

## North Star

Upgrade MarkOS into a future-proof, deeply researched, universally applicable, and highly tailorable marketing and digital business literacy system that produces output aligned to both:

1. the company being served, and  
2. the ICP's deepest pain points, needs, motivations, and natural decision psychology.

The milestone should harden MarkOS so prompt generation, agent behavior, and delivered marketing content feel clearly superior for 2026+ quality standards rather than generic or shallow.

## Core Intent From Discussion

The user wants this milestone to push beyond ordinary personalization. MarkOS should not only tailor to business facts, but also to the human reality of the ICP:

- what the ICP is trying to achieve
- what they fear losing
- what friction, doubt, or urgency they feel
- what language feels natural to them
- what neurological and emotional triggers influence trust and action

Neuromarketing is a built-in differentiator and must be treated as a first-class system capability, not an optional add-on.

## Locked Milestone Priorities

### 1. Dual-layer tailoring is mandatory
Every strong output must be tailored to:
- the specific company and offer
- the ICP segment and its main pain points, needs, objections, motivations, and desired outcomes

### 2. Neuromarketing must be embedded across the system
Neuromarketing should live in:
- the literacy corpus
- the MarkOS training and instruction layers
- agent behavior and skills
- evaluation and QA criteria

This should align with the existing MarkOS neuro reference model, including biological triggers, archetypes, funnel-stage mapping, and natural decision framing.

### 3. Literacy must be templatized but not generic
The literacy base should become:
- reusable across many industries and business models
- structured enough for deterministic retrieval and composition
- rich enough to feel custom, specific, and valuable when applied to a real company + ICP pair

### 4. Future-quality bar must be explicit
This milestone should raise the baseline so MarkOS output is competitive with or better than advanced 2026 marketing systems in:
- relevance
- naturality
- persuasion quality
- specificity
- strategic depth
- trustworthiness
- audience fit

### 5. Tailoring must influence generation, not just retrieval
The system should improve the actual thinking and drafting behavior of MarkOS agents so they consistently:
- select the right angle
- emphasize the right pains and outcomes
- use the right neuro triggers and proof style
- avoid shallow or template-sounding messaging

## Proposed Scope Areas

### A. Advanced literacy architecture upgrade
Expand the literacy system so each artifact can encode:
- ICP pain points and job-to-be-done context
- desired transformation and emotional state shift
- objection patterns and trust blockers
- business-model and industry nuance
- funnel stage and buying maturity
- neuromarketing trigger alignment
- messaging naturality and tone expectations

### B. Universal-but-tailorable template library
Create richer literacy templates that work across:
- B2B
- B2C
- service businesses
- agencies
- SaaS
- info-product and consulting offers
- ecommerce / product-led offers

These templates should still adapt strongly based on company, offer, ICP, and stage.

### C. ICP neuro-intelligence layer
Formalize a reusable system for mapping ICPs to:
- pains
- motivations
- fears
- trust drivers
- objections
- decision patterns
- likely biological trigger fits
- psychological archetypes or identity cues

### D. Agent-training and skill alignment
Upgrade MarkOS agents and skills so they consistently consume and apply the new literacy logic rather than ignoring it.

This includes:
- prompt scaffolds
- planning expectations
- generation heuristics
- review criteria
- operator-facing explanations of why a message angle was chosen

### E. Quality and evaluation framework
Add a measurable quality bar for:
- personalization depth
- ICP fit
- neuro-trigger appropriateness
- naturality and non-generic language
- evidence grounding
- conversion-oriented usefulness

## Non-Negotiable Product Behaviors

The milestone should make it normal for MarkOS to generate different outputs for:
- the same company with different ICP segments
- the same ICP at different funnel stages
- the same offer under different competitive or trust conditions
- different industries sharing a business model but needing different emotional framing

The system should explain not just what it generated, but why the chosen message direction fits that audience.

## Success Definition

This milestone is successful when MarkOS can reliably produce output that is:
- deeply tailored to the company context
- deeply tailored to ICP pain points and motivations
- visibly more human, natural, and persuasive
- grounded in reusable literacy rather than ad hoc prompting
- consistent across agents, prompts, and review surfaces
- defensible as a premium differentiator for MarkOS

## Suggested Milestone Themes for Roadmapping

1. Neuro-aware literacy schema and taxonomy expansion
2. Universal template and business-model coverage upgrade
3. ICP pain-point and motivation intelligence layer
4. Agent and skill training alignment for tailored generation
5. Evaluation and governance for future-grade output quality

## Guardrails

- Do not regress the v3.6 deep research and governance foundations.
- Do not allow neuromarketing to become manipulative fluff; it must remain evidence-aware, relevant, and brand-safe.
- Do not optimize only for persuasion while losing truthfulness, clarity, or operator trust.
- Do not keep personalization at the metadata layer only; it must affect actual copy and strategy output.
