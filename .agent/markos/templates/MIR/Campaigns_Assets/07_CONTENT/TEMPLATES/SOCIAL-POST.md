## Instagram / Facebook — Text Post or Caption

```
HOOK (Line 1 — visible before "more"):
[One sentence. Problem, bold claim, or question. 10 words max.]

[Line break]

BODY (3–7 short paragraphs):
[Each paragraph = 1–3 lines. White space is structural.]
[One idea per paragraph.]
[Build from hook → insight → proof → implication → CTA]

[Line break]

CTA:
[Direct. One action. No multiple options.]

HASHTAGS (Instagram only — if used):
[3–5 highly relevant. Never stuffed. End of caption, not in body.]
```

---

## Instagram Reel — Hook Script Structure

```
HOOK (0–3 sec): 
[Visual hook + audio hook must work independently]
[Text overlay: [Statement or question — 6 words max]]
[Verbal: [Same or complementary — spoken in first 2 sec]]

CONTENT (3–45 sec):
[Deliver on the hook. Teach, show, or prove one thing.]
[Structure: Point → Example → So what]

CTA (last 5 sec):
[One action. Make it low-friction for organic.]
[Audio: verbal CTA]
[Text overlay: reinforce CTA]
```

---

## LinkedIn — Professional Audience Post

```
HOOK (Line 1 — must stand alone):
[Counterintuitive claim, specific data point, or strong opinion]

BODY:
[Para 1: Set the context — 2–3 sentences]
[Para 2: The insight or lesson — the "so what"]
[Para 3: Evidence or example — specific, not generic]
[Para 4: Implication for the reader — what should they do with this]

CTA:
[Comment prompt, question, or link — not all three]
```

## Platform-Specific Rules

| Platform | Max Characters | Hashtags | Emoji | Links |
|----------|---------------|---------|-------|-------|
| Instagram caption | 2,200 | 3–5, end of caption | Allowed | No (link in bio) |
| Facebook | 63,206 | 1–2 or none | Allowed | Yes |
| LinkedIn | 3,000 | 1–3 or none | Sparingly | Yes (in comment ideally) |
| X (Twitter) | 280 / 25,000 premium | 1–2 | Sparingly | Yes |
| TikTok | 2,200 | 3–5 | Allowed | No |

## Post Types by Content Pillar

| Pillar | Best Post Types | Hook Pattern |
|--------|----------------|-------------|
| C1 — Educational | Carousel, text post, Reel tutorial | "Most people don't know that..." |
| C2 — Social Proof | Screenshot, testimonial quote, results | "Client went from X to Y in N weeks" |
| C3 — Behind-the-scenes | Story, Reel, text | "Here's exactly how we..." |
| C4 — Promotional | Story, feed post, Reel | "Last [X] spots / [time-limited offer]" |
