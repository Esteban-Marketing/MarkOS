## Lead Generation Page Structure

```
SECTION 1: HERO
  - Pre-headline (optional): [Small text above — audience qualifier or proof hook]
  - Headline (H1): [Primary outcome claim — 8–12 words max]
  - Sub-headline (H2): [Support the headline with mechanism or specificity]
  - Hero CTA: [Single button — action + benefit — 5 words max]
  - Hero visual: [Spec per VISUAL-GUIDELINES.md]
  - Trust signal row: [Logo bar, review stars, or client count — below CTA]

SECTION 2: PROBLEM AGITATION
  - Section headline: [Name the problem clearly]
  - Problem description: [3–5 bullets — specifics, not generics]
  - Consequence: [What happens if they don't solve this]

SECTION 3: MECHANISM / SOLUTION
  - How we solve it: [The process or method — 3 steps max]
  - What makes this different: [Single differentiator — reference MESSAGING-FRAMEWORK.md]

SECTION 4: PROOF
  - Primary social proof type: [Testimonial / Case study / Stats]
  - Minimum 2 proof elements at this stage
  - Each proof must include: Name, title/company, specific result

SECTION 5: OFFER
  - What they get: [Specific deliverables or outcome]
  - Who it's for: [ICP qualifier — help them self-select]
  - Who it's NOT for: [Anti-qualifier — prevents unqualified leads]

SECTION 6: CTA BLOCK
  - Headline: [Restate the primary benefit]
  - Form or button: [Minimum required fields: Name, Email, Phone (optional)]
  - Risk reversal: [What removes the fear of clicking]
  - Privacy statement: [Reference LEGAL.md for compliant text]

SECTION 7: FAQ (optional)
  - 4–6 questions from MESSAGING-FRAMEWORK.md §8
  - Each answer ≤ 3 sentences

FOOTER:
  - Logo
  - Privacy Policy link (required)
  - Terms of Service link (required)
  - Copyright notice
```

## Thank You Page Structure

```
HEADLINE: Confirm the action taken
SUB-HEADLINE: Set next step expectation (when / what / how)
CONTENT: What happens next (numbered — 2–3 steps)
SECONDARY CTA (optional): Lower-friction offer or resource
SOCIAL PROOF: Reinforce their decision with a testimonial
```
