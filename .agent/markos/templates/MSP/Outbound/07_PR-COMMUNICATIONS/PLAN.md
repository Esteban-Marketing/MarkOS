> PR generates credibility that paid media cannot buy. A mention in a trusted publication
> reaches an audience that will never click a paid ad. It also feeds SEO (backlinks),
> social proof (screenshot of coverage), and sales (name-drop in proposals).
> PR is a slow-burn discipline. Expect 60–90 days from pitch to publication.
