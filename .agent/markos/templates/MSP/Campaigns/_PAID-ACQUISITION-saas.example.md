# Reference Example: **SaaS** (Developer Observability Platform)

---

## Phase Budget & Parameters
- **Target CAC Limits:** $3,200 (blended; all paid channels)
- **Target CPA Bounds:** $180 trial start; $1,400 trial-to-paid conversion cost
- **LTV Goal:** $28,000 ARR per account (avg 3-year retention × $9,200 ACV)
- **Daily Spend Threshold:** $280; scale after trial→paid cohort data shows >20% conversion

## 1. Audience Matrix

**Google Search (Primary — Intent Capture):**
- Problem keywords: "alert fatigue observability", "reduce MTTR engineering", "distributed tracing tool"
- Competitor keywords: "[Datadog/New Relic/Grafana] alternative", "cheaper than Datadog for startups"
- Evaluation keywords: "best observability platform 2025", "OpenTelemetry compatible tools"
- Negative: "free open source observability" — wrong CAC profile

**LinkedIn (Economic Buyer Nurture — Secondary):**
- Titles: VP Engineering, Director of Infrastructure, SRE Lead, CTO (startup/scaleup)
- Company size: 50–2000 employees (below has no budget; above too long a sales cycle)
- Industry: SaaS, fintech, e-commerce, developer tools — not manufacturing, healthcare, government

**Retargeting:**
- Trial started, no integration in 48h → nurture email sequence (also eligible for LinkedIn retarget: "Most teams get their first alert in under 10 minutes — did you need help?")
- Visited pricing page, no trial → "Price looks fair when you calculate your current MTTR cost" creative
- Docs visitors (high intent signal) → demo request ad

## 2. Pipeline Execution: Google Search Ads (Primary)

**Campaign 1 — Competitor Conquest:**
- Keywords: "[Competitor] alternative", "[Competitor] pricing", "[Competitor] vs [your brand]"
- Landing page: Dedicated comparison page with honest side-by-side + "Try free for 14 days"
- Bid: $6–$12 CPC (worth it — highest intent)
- KPI: CTR >5%, trial start rate >8%

**Campaign 2 — Problem Intent:**
- Keywords: "reduce MTTR", "alert fatigue solution", "observability for microservices"
- Landing page: Specific pain-point page with case study (e.g. "How Acme cut MTTR by 40%") + trial CTA
- Bid: $4–$8 CPC
- KPI: CTR >3%, trial start rate >5%

**Campaign 3 — Branded Defense:**
- Exact match: company name + "pricing", "review", "login"
- Budget: $30/day — pure defense
- KPI: Impression share >90%

## 3. Pipeline Execution: LinkedIn Ads (Economic Buyer Reach)

**Sponsored Content:**
- Format: Document ads ("The Real Cost of Alert Fatigue — 2025 Engineering Survey")
- Audience: VP Engineering, 100–1000 employee SaaS companies
- CTA: "Download the report" → email capture → SDR follow-up sequence
- Budget: 30% of LinkedIn allocation
- KPI: Download rate >8%, CPL <$180

**Retargeting — Proof Layer:**
- Format: Company logo carousel showing enterprise customers + 1 sentence each
- Copy: "The engineering teams at [X, Y, Z] reduced MTTR by 40% with [brand]."
- CTA: Demo request
- KPI: Demo request rate >1.5%

## 4. Optimization Loop

- Trial starts with 0 integrations at Day 3 → SDR outreach + paid retarget "Did you need help getting set up?"
- Trial starts with integration but no dashboard build at Day 7 → in-app nudge + email sequence
- Kill any Google ad group with CPC >1.5x target and <3% conversion rate after 200 clicks
- Scale any competitor campaign with trial→paid rate >18% by 30% budget increase weekly

## Quick Wins (First 14 Days)

1. **Launch competitor comparison page + Google campaign first** — fastest pipeline signal at this stage
2. **Set up trial activation email sequence before increasing ad spend** — wasted spend if trial users have no activation path
3. **Identify top 3 competitor migration queries in Search Console** from organic — run paid on those exact terms

## Channels to Avoid (For Now)

- Meta/Instagram: SaaS evaluators are not in purchase-evaluation mode on consumer social
- Display retargeting (broad): Low signal-to-noise; use LinkedIn retarget instead for this audience
- YouTube pre-roll: Long creative cycle; only when video library includes proper technical demos (6+ months in)
