# Reference Example: **B2C** (Premium Home Fitness Brand)

---

## Phase Budget & Parameters
- **Target CAC Limits:** $38 blended (first purchase)
- **Target CPA Bounds:** $28 purchase event; $4 add-to-cart
- **LTV Goal:** $290 (3 purchases/year target)
- **Daily Spend Threshold:** $180 (scale 20% every 72h if ROAS >3x)

## 1. Audience Matrix

**Cold Audiences (Meta):**
- Interest: Home gym equipment, fitness technology, boutique fitness, yoga, CrossFit
- Behavioral: "Engaged shoppers" + "Health & fitness" interest overlap
- Demographic: 28–48, income index >1.5 (affluent), homeowners preferred

**Lookalike Audiences:**
- 1% LAL from: purchasers with 2+ orders (best LTV signal)
- 1% LAL from: 60-day email openers
- 3% LAL from: full customer list (volume expansion)

**Retargeting Layers:**
- 0–3 day abandonment: urgency creative ("Still thinking it over? This is still available.")
- 4–7 day: social proof creative (reviews + UGC)
- 8–14 day: offer (10% first-order code, expires 48h)
- 15–30 day: re-engagement angle (new content piece or collection drop)

## 2. Pipeline Execution: Meta Ads (Primary Performance Channel)

**Sprint 1 — Top of Funnel (Cold Audiences):**
- Format: 15–30s video Reel (aspirational lifestyle, product in natural home environment)
- Angle A: "Replace your $200/month gym membership" — economic value message
- Angle B: "Used by 12,000 home athletes" — social proof scale
- CTA: "Shop now" → best-selling product page
- Budget: 55% of Meta budget
- KPI: ROAS >2.5x, Thumb-stop rate >35%

**Sprint 2 — Middle of Funnel (Site Visitors, no purchase):**
- Format: Carousel (3 slides: product hero, key feature, 5-star review)
- Copy: "You were checking this out. Here's what 847 customers are saying:"
- CTA: "Add to cart" → direct product page
- Budget: 30% of Meta budget
- KPI: ROAS >4x, add-to-cart rate >6%

**Sprint 3 — Bottom of Funnel (ATC, no purchase):**
- Format: Single image + offer text overlay
- Copy: "Complete your order — your [product] is reserved." + 10% expiry timer
- CTA: "Claim discount"
- Budget: 15% of Meta budget
- KPI: ROAS >6x, purchase rate >15% of ATC audience

## 3. Pipeline Execution: Google Shopping

- **Product Feed Quality:** Ensure all titles include primary keyword + product variant (e.g. "Home Pull-Up Bar — Wall-Mounted, 400lb Capacity")
- **Smart Shopping:** Off. Run Standard Shopping campaigns with manual CPC control.
- **Branded Search:** Defensive — $25/day exact match only.
- **Non-branded search:** Do not run until ROAS from Meta is stable >3x — too competitive at this stage.

## 4. Creative Refresh Cadence

- Refresh creative every 14 days or when CTR drops >20% from baseline (whichever first)
- Always test 3 video angles simultaneously — never go single-creative
- UGC > polished studio at every funnel stage for this audience

## Quick Wins (First 14 Days)

1. **Launch cart abandonment sequence immediately** (email + SMS) — this is the highest-ROAS lever before any paid spend
2. **Upload customer email list to Meta as Custom Audience** before launching cold campaigns — enables immediate 1% LAL seeding
3. **Film one authentic "unboxing in real home" video** with a real customer — this creative type consistently outperforms studio content for home fitness

## Channels to Avoid (For Now)

- LinkedIn: Wrong audience mode for consumer product
- TikTok Ads (paid): Organic TikTok first to validate content angles; paid TikTok only after 3+ organic videos >50K views
- Pinterest: Only after Meta ROAS is proven stable and creative library is deep enough to adapt
