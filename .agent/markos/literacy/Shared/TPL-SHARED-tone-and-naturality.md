# Shared Tone and Naturality Guidance

Use the same strategic backbone across every business model, but let the voice shift with the buyer's current level of trust, attention, and urgency.

## EVIDENCE BASE
- Brand-safe naturality improves comprehension and reduces sales resistance when the message sounds like a thoughtful operator rather than a template.
- Tone should become more specific and proof-backed as the buyer moves from Awareness toward Decision.

## CORE TACTICS
### Awareness
Lead with empathy, category clarity, and the pain the buyer already feels. Avoid hard closes and abstract jargon.

### Consideration
Increase specificity. Show the mechanism, the differentiator, and the trade-offs in plain language.

### Decision
Use confident but evidence-led tone. Clarify the next step, reduce uncertainty, and surface real-world proof.

### Onboarding
Sound clear and calming. Remove ambiguity, explain the first win, and reduce activation friction.

### Retention
Reinforce value with concise reminders, credible wins, and supportive language rather than novelty for novelty's sake.

## COUNTER-INDICATORS
- Overheated urgency when the buyer is still in Awareness.
- Empty polish without proof posture.
- Robotic listicles that flatten the naturality of the message.
