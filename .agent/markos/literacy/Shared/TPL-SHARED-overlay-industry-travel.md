# Travel & Hospitality Overlay

Use this overlay when the audience is in destination-aspiration or trip-planning mode. The travel buyer's emotional arc starts with desire and ends with booking confidence — content must move them through both. Review-gate behavior is high in this vertical: social proof and star ratings are purchase prerequisites, not nice-to-haves. Seasonal urgency is a legitimate lever when authentic; manufactured scarcity destroys trust quickly with experienced travelers.
