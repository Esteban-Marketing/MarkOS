# Marketing Services Overlay

Use this overlay when the buyer is a CMO, VP of Marketing, or growth-oriented founder evaluating an external marketing partner. Performance metrics are the native language of this buyer — lead with results, not process. Differentiate by demonstrating data fluency and attribution clarity. The commoditization risk in this vertical is high; proof of unique methodology or measurably differentiated outcomes is the primary trust lever.
