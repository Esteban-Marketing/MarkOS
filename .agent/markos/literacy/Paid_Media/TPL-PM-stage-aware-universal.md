# Paid Media Universal Template

This universal paid media base keeps the message stage-aware and business-model-flexible without turning into generic ad copy.

## EVIDENCE BASE
- Cold traffic needs clarity and pattern interruption, not dense proof or premature detail.
- Warm traffic responds better to concrete mechanism, credible differentiation, and objection handling.

## CORE TACTICS
### Awareness
Open with the pain, tension, or missed opportunity the buyer already recognizes.

### Consideration
Clarify the mechanism and why this approach beats the current default.

### Decision
Tighten the CTA and support it with offer clarity, real constraints, and decision-stage proof.

### Onboarding
Reduce friction around the first action so the click has a clear next step.

### Retention
Use ads to reinforce value, reactivate usage, or surface a new expansion moment.
