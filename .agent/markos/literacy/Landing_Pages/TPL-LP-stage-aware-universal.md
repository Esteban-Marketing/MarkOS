# Landing Page Universal Template

The page should guide the buyer from recognition to trust to action with as little friction as possible. The structure stays reusable, while the proof and CTA strength flex with the stage and business model.

## EVIDENCE BASE
- Landing pages convert better when they move from problem clarity to mechanism to proof to next step.
- Proof posture needs to match the risk of the decision and the commitment being asked.

## CORE TACTICS
### Awareness
Name the pain clearly and reduce confusion.

### Consideration
Explain the mechanism and what makes this approach credible.

### Decision
Use outcome proof, objection handling, and commitment-reducing CTA language.

### Onboarding and Retention
Make the next step feel safe, clear, and progress-oriented.
