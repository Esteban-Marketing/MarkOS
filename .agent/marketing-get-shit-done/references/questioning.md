# Questioning — Marketing Discovery Questions

Techniques and context checklist for `/mgsd-discuss-phase` and `/mgsd-new-project`.

## Opening Question

"What marketing challenge are you trying to solve?"

Wait for response. Then follow the thread.

## Follow-Up Techniques

### Challenge Vagueness
- "You said 'grow the business' — grow what specifically? Revenue? Leads? Brand awareness?"
- "What does 'better marketing' look like in numbers?"

### Make Abstract Concrete
- "Who is the actual person seeing this ad? What are they doing when they see it?"
- "What would a customer say to a friend about your product?"

### Surface Assumptions
- "You're assuming Meta Ads will work — what evidence supports that?"
- "Is the audience actually on that platform?"

### Find Edges
- "What's the maximum you'd spend per lead before it's not worth it?"
- "What would make you kill this campaign early?"

### Reveal Motivation
- "Why this channel? Why now?"
- "What happened that made this urgent?"

## Context Checklist (Background, Not Out Loud)

As you gather information, mentally check these areas. If gaps remain, weave questions naturally. Don't switch to checklist mode.

### Business Identity
- [ ] What the company does (product/service)
- [ ] Target market (B2B / B2C / hybrid)
- [ ] Current revenue stage (pre-revenue / growing / established)
- [ ] Geographic focus

### Audience
- [ ] Primary ICP defined (at least demographics + psychographics)
- [ ] Where the audience spends time online
- [ ] What language they use to describe the problem
- [ ] Current customer acquisition cost (if known)

### Brand & Voice
- [ ] Brand voice exists (formal / casual / authoritative / playful)
- [ ] Prohibited words or topics
- [ ] Competitive positioning (how they differentiate)

### Channels & Budget
- [ ] Which marketing channels are active today
- [ ] Monthly/quarterly marketing budget
- [ ] Previous campaigns (what worked, what didn't)
- [ ] Tech stack (analytics, automation, CRM)

### Goals & KPIs
- [ ] Primary metric (leads, sales, awareness, retention)
- [ ] Target numbers (monthly lead goal, CAC target, ROAS target)
- [ ] Timeline (when do results need to show)

### Constraints
- [ ] Regulatory constraints (health, finance, legal)
- [ ] Brand guidelines (colors, fonts, imagery rules)
- [ ] Approval workflows (who signs off on creative, copy, budget)

## Decision Gate

When you could write a clear PROJECT.md or campaign brief:

```
AskUserQuestion:
  header: "Ready?"
  question: "I have enough context. Ready to proceed?"
  options:
    - "Create PROJECT.md" — Let's formalize
    - "Keep exploring" — I want to share more
```
