## Meta Feed Ad — Cold Audience

```
HOOK (Line 1 — 5–10 words):
[Problem statement or outcome claim — no brand mention]

BODY (2–4 sentences):
[Problem agitation → introduce mechanism → outcome]
[Specific, not vague. One idea per sentence.]

SOCIAL PROOF (1 sentence, optional):
[Specific result, client count, or testimonial fragment]

CTA (5 words max):
[Single action — no multiple options]

HEADLINE (below creative — 6 words max):
[Outcome-focused. Not a slogan.]

DESCRIPTION (25 chars max):
[Supporting detail or objection handle]
```

---

## Meta Retargeting Ad — Warm Audience

```
HOOK: Reference their previous interaction without being creepy
[e.g. "Still thinking about [topic]?"]

BODY: Address the #1 objection for this audience
[Use specific objection + specific counter from MESSAGING-FRAMEWORK.md]

PROOF: Most relevant proof point for this stage
[Testimonial, case study, or specific metric]

CTA: Lower friction than cold — they know you
[e.g. "Ver opciones" vs "Descubrir más"]
```

## Google Search Ad

```
HEADLINE 1 (30 chars): [Primary keyword — match intent]
HEADLINE 2 (30 chars): [Key benefit or differentiator]
HEADLINE 3 (30 chars): [CTA or proof point]

DESCRIPTION 1 (90 chars): [Expand on benefit. Address objection.]
DESCRIPTION 2 (90 chars): [Proof point + CTA]

PATH 1 (15 chars): [Keyword-relevant]
PATH 2 (15 chars): [Benefit or category]
```
