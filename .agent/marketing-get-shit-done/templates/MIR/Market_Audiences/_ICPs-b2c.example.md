## 1. Ideal Consumer Profile (ICP) Overview

The ideal customer is a **high-achieving professional**, most commonly a woman aged 30–45, who earns above the household median, values both her physical and mental performance, and has a history of spending on wellness products. She has an existing fitness habit (gym, yoga, running) and is seeking a home solution that fits her schedule without sacrificing quality. She researches purchases thoroughly and relies on peer reviews and expert endorsements before committing to premium price points.

---

## 2. Consumer Profile Attributes

| Dimension | Ideal Value | Acceptable Range |
|-----------|-------------|------------------|
| Age | 30 – 45 | 25 – 55 |
| Gender | Female (primary); Gender-inclusive for secondary segment | All genders |
| Household Income | $100K+ | $75K+ |
| Location | Urban / Suburban US metros | US, Canada, UK, Australia |
| Fitness Baseline | Active 3+ times/week (gym, running, yoga) | Any established exercise habit |
| Prior Spend on Fitness | >$500/yr on gym + gear | Any fitness-related purchase in past 12 months |
| Home Setup | Has dedicated workout space or clear floor space | Any home setup |
| Tech Comfort | Streams content, uses fitness apps (Peloton, Whoop, Apple Watch) | Owns a smartphone |
| Purchase Trigger | Life event, health goal, or peer influence | Any motivational catalyst |
| Lifetime Value Potential | High repeat buyer (supplements, accessories, apparel) | At minimum: equipment repurchase within 5 years |

---

## 3. Disqualifiers (Do Not Target)

* Consumers with no fitness habit or no intent to build one — no exercise motivation = no retention.
* Price-conscious shoppers primarily on Amazon looking for the cheapest option — not the right fit for premium positioning.
* Customers who live in small apartments with no usable floor space — product won't fit their lifestyle.
* Markets without shipping access to bulky equipment within reasonable cost parameters.
