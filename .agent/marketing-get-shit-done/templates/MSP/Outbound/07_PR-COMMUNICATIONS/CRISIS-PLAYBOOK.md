> A crisis is any event that could damage the brand, client relationships,
> or {{LEAD_AGENT}}'s personal reputation if not handled quickly and correctly.
> Speed and consistency are the two most important elements of crisis response.

---

## Crisis Classification

| Level | Definition | Response Time | Owner |
|-------|-----------|-------------|-------|
| LOW | Negative review, minor social criticism | 4 business hours | {{LEAD_AGENT}} |
| MEDIUM | Platform ban, tracking failure, client complaint | 2 hours | {{LEAD_AGENT}} |
| HIGH | Legal threat, data breach, media inquiry | Immediate | {{LEAD_AGENT}} + Legal |
