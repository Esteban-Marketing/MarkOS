# Reference Example: **Agents-aaS** (AI Marketing Operations Agent Platform)

---

## Phase Budget & Parameters
- **Target CAC Limits:** $4,800 blended (long cycle; high LTV justifies)
- **Target CPA Bounds:** $420 demo request; $1,800 trial activation; $4,800 closed deal
- **LTV Goal:** $78,000 ARR per account (avg 3-year retention × $26,000 ACV)
- **Daily Spend Threshold:** $220; do NOT scale until trial-to-paid rate >30% (quality gate before volume)

## 1. Audience Matrix

**Google Search (Intent — Primary):**
- "AI marketing automation platform"
- "autonomous marketing agent"
- "marketing operations AI tool"
- "replace marketing ops with AI"
- Competitor-adjacent: "HubSpot AI alternative", "Marketo autonomous workflows"
- Evaluation searches: "AI marketing platform review 2025", "best AI for CMO"
- Negative: "AI content writing tool" (Jasper-intent — wrong use case), "free AI tool for marketing"

**LinkedIn (Economic Buyer — Primary):**
- Titles: CMO, VP Marketing, Head of Growth, Director of Marketing Operations, RevOps Lead
- Company Size: 50–500 employees (smaller lacks budget; larger has internal AI teams)
- Industry: SaaS, fintech, e-commerce, B2B tech
- Signal targeting: Job postings for "Marketing Operations Manager" (signal = ops headcount gap → your product solves)

**Retargeting:**
- Demo page visitors, no request (Day 0–3): security/compliance reassurance creative ("Your data never trains our models")
- Demo requested, no trial activation (Day 7+): SDR outreach + retarget "Setup takes 20 minutes. Here's a Loom walkthrough."
- Trial started, no agent run (Day 3): in-app nudge + LinkedIn retarget "Your first agent run is ready"

## 2. Pipeline Execution: Google Search

**Campaign 1 — Category Intent:**
- Keywords: "AI marketing agent", "autonomous marketing operations"
- Landing: Product overview page with 3-minute demo video + social proof bar (company logos)
- CTA: "Watch 3-minute demo" (lower friction than "request demo" for cold intent)
- Budget: 40% of search budget
- KPI: Demo watch rate >35%, CTA click rate >4%

**Campaign 2 — Competitor Displacement:**
- Keywords: "HubSpot too manual", "Marketo alternative autonomous", "marketing ops without headcount"
- Landing: ROI comparison page — "X hrs/week your team spends doing what our agent handles"
- CTA: "Request personalized ROI estimate"
- Budget: 35% of search budget
- KPI: Form fill rate >3%, CPL <$280

**Campaign 3 — Evaluation/Research:**
- Keywords: "AI marketing platform comparison", "best marketing automation 2025"
- Landing: G2 review page or third-party comparison article (outrank with paid until organic ranks)
- Budget: 25% of search budget
- KPI: G2 page click-through >12%

## 3. Pipeline Execution: LinkedIn Ads

**Sponsored Content — Thought Leadership:**
- Format: Native article ad — "What happened when we ran 10,000 email send decisions through our agent (and where we added human gates)"
- Audience: CMOs, VPs Marketing at SaaS companies 50–500
- CTA: "Read the full breakdown"
- Budget: 50% LinkedIn allocation
- KPI: Article read rate >30%, click-to-demo rate >2%

**Document Ad — Technical Proof:**
- Format: "Agent Architecture Overview — SOC 2, data flow, audit logs" (gated download)
- Audience: CTOs, VP Engineering, IT Leads at same companies (technical co-buyer)
- CTA: "Download architecture overview"
- Budget: 30% LinkedIn allocation
- KPI: Download rate >8%, share to colleague rate (track UTM)

**Retargeting — Proof of Product:**
- Format: 90-second product demo video (unedited screen capture of agent running real workflow)
- Copy: "This is what autonomous marketing actually looks like in production."
- Budget: 20% LinkedIn allocation
- KPI: Watch-to-end rate >40%, demo request rate >1.5% of viewers

## 4. Optimization Loop

- Day 7: If demo fill rate <2% on all campaigns → headline test (3 variants) before increasing spend
- Day 14: If trial activation <25% of demos booked → fix onboarding before scaling leads
- Day 21: Calculate CAC by channel; kill any source >2x target CAC with <5 trials in period
- Bi-weekly: Review demo quality score with sales team — rank channels by SQL rate, not demo volume

## Quick Wins (First 14 Days)

1. **Publish "Live Agent Session" demo video** (unedited, authentic) on LinkedIn + use as paid ad creative — highest trust signal for AI-skeptic buyers
2. **Launch competitor displacement Google campaign Day 1** — fastest pipeline signal, most qualified intent
3. **Get 10 G2 reviews from existing users** this week — G2 visibility threshold crossed at ~20 reviews; below that, spending on evaluation-intent search keywords is wasted

## Channels to Avoid

- Meta/Instagram ads: CMO budget decisions don't happen during Instagram browsing
- TikTok: Wrong context and audience maturity for $26K ACV contract evaluation
- Broad LinkedIn Awareness (reach campaigns): Waste for this CAC/LTV profile; every dollar must be accountable to pipeline
- Cold email at scale: Actively harms brand trust for an AI company — ironic and self-defeating
