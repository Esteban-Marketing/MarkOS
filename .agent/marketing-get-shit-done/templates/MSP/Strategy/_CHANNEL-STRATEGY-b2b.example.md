# Reference Example: **B2B** (HR Technology — Applicant Tracking)

<!-- mgsd-token: MSP | model: B2B -->
> [!NOTE] B2B channel strategy is content-led and community-driven. Paid acquisition is secondary — trust is built through education and peer proof, not reach.

---

## Channel Priority Stack

**1. LinkedIn (Organic + Paid)**
- Why this channel: HR Directors and VPs of People Ops live on LinkedIn. It's where they consume industry news, validate vendor reputation, and make professional buying signals visible (new job posting = new headcount = new software need).
- Content format priority: Original data posts ("What 200 HR leaders told us about hiring velocity"), short video walkthroughs, case study reposts, thought leadership articles (800–1,500 words).
- Posting frequency target: 4–5 posts/week on company page; 3–5 posts/week on founder/executive personal page.
- KPI to track: Follower growth (>5%/month), engagement rate (>3%), LinkedIn-sourced demo booked.

**2. Long-Form Content / SEO Blog**
- Why: HR buyers search Google when they have a specific problem ("how to reduce time-to-hire," "ATS comparison 2025"). Being ranked on these queries puts us in front of buyers in the evaluation stage.
- Content format: 1,500–3,000 word pillar articles, ATS comparison guides, compliance checklists. Include downloadable templates (ROI calculator, hiring audit template) as lead magnets.
- Posting frequency: 2–3 articles/week.
- KPI: Organic traffic growth, #1–3 rankings for 5 priority keywords, email list signups from content.

**3. Email Newsletter (Owned Audience)**
- Why: Email is the only channel we fully own. A list of 10,000 engaged HR practitioners is worth more than 100,000 LinkedIn followers.
- Content format: Weekly "People Ops Brief" — 3 curated articles + 1 original insight + 1 tool tip. Not a sales newsletter.
- Posting frequency: 1x/week.
- KPI: 40%+ open rate, <0.2% unsubscribe rate, demo conversion from list monthly.

**4. Webinars / Virtual Events**
- Why: HR buyers attend virtual events for CPE credits, peer learning, and vendor evaluation. A branded webinar positions us as the educator — not just a vendor.
- Content format: Monthly 45-minute webinar with a guest practitioner (not just our team). Panel format outperforms solo product demos.
- Posting frequency: 1 webinar/month, replayed as on-demand content.
- KPI: Registrations (>300 per event), attendance rate (>40%), pipeline generated per event.

**5. Partner / Integration Co-Marketing**
- Why: HR tech buyers choose tools that integrate with their existing stack. A co-marketing partnership with BambooHR or Greenhouse puts us in front of their installed base.
- Content format: Co-branded integration guides, joint customer case studies, partner-promoted webinars.
- Posting frequency: 1 co-marketing asset per partner per quarter.
- KPI: Partner-sourced MQLs, integration page traffic.

## Quick Wins (First 30 Days)

1. **LinkedIn Personal Brand Activation**: Publish 5 posts from the CEO/Founder account sharing specific data from customer conversations (anonymized). No product pitching — pure practitioner insight. Target: 500+ impressions per post within first week.
2. **ICP-Targeted Blog Post**: Publish one deeply researched article targeting the #1 Google search term for the buying intent persona ("best ATS for fast-growing companies" type keyword). Optimize for featured snippet.
3. **Customer Story Email**: Write and send one email to the current customer list featuring a specific customer win (with their permission). Include exact metrics — not vague testimonials. Measure open rate and forward rate.

## Channels to Explicitly Avoid (For Now)

- **TikTok / Instagram Reels**: Our audience is not discovering HR tech vendors on short-form video. Brand awareness spend here cannot be tracked to pipeline.
- **Facebook (Organic)**: HR professional audience engagement on Facebook is low. Ad targeting exists but not worth the effort while LinkedIn is untapped.
- **Podcast (Own Show)**: Building a podcast audience takes 12+ months. Not a quick-win channel — evaluate at Month 9 if content engine is mature.
