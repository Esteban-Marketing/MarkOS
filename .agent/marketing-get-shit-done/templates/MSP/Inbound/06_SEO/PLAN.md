> SEO is the most patient discipline in marketing. It takes 6–12 months to show meaningful
> results, and its compounding returns outlast any paid campaign.
> It is not optional for businesses that want cost-efficient lead generation at scale.
> It is not appropriate as a primary channel in Year 1 when lead flow is urgent.
