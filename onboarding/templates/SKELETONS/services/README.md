# Services / Consulting Starter Skeleton

Business model: **Professional Services / Consulting**

## When to use this skeleton

Use this starter pack for businesses that sell expertise, time, and outcomes through service engagements. The primary conversion mechanic is trust and relationship development, not transaction or trial. Applies to:

- Management consultancies and strategy firms
- Agency and creative services
- Fractional executive services (CFO, CMO, CTO)
- Independent consultants and solo practitioners
- Specialized technical or professional services

## How services content differs from B2B product content

B2B product content earns trial → conversion on a technical purchasing path. Services content earns relationship → proposal invitation on a trust-building path. The buyer isn't evaluating a product specification; they are evaluating whether this firm understands their specific situation and has made them feel confident the firm can handle it.

Key mechanics this skeleton addresses:
- LinkedIn thought leadership as primary acquisition channel
- Case study and process transparency as conversion tools
- Low-commitment CTAs (discovery call, free audit, strategy session)
- Proposal follow-up and nurture for long decision cycles
- Relationship-sustaining content for existing clients

## Tone guidance

See [TPL-SHARED-business-model-services.md](../../../../.agent/markos/literacy/Shared/TPL-SHARED-business-model-services.md) for full tone doc.

Summary: Senior, human, practitioner-peer voice. Advice-giver tone, never salesperson tone. Trust is built by demonstrating understanding and expertise — not by promoting. Urgency language is strictly counter-indicated. Every asset should help the prospect understand if this firm is the right fit, not pressure them toward a commitment.
