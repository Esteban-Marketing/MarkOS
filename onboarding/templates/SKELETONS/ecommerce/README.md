# Ecommerce Starter Skeleton

Business model: **Ecommerce / DTC / Online Retail**

## When to use this skeleton

Use this starter pack for brands that sell physical or digital products through an online store. The primary conversion mechanic is the product purchase transaction. Applies to:

- Direct-to-consumer (DTC) brands
- Multi-brand online retailers
- Consumer packaged goods (CPG) with a D2C channel
- Subscription product boxes
- Marketplace-first brands building owned channels

## How ecommerce content differs from general B2C

General B2C content builds brand relationship over time. Ecommerce content converts at the transaction level: it makes the right product visible, credible, and purchasable with low friction. Review volume, product visual quality, and checkout confidence signals matter more than brand voice consistency in the short run.

Key mechanics this skeleton addresses:
- ROAS-optimized paid creative (Meta DPA, Google Shopping)
- Product listing and category SEO
- Cart abandonment and post-purchase email sequences
- Product page conversion psychology
- Social UGC and product-proof formats
