# Travel & Hospitality Industry Overlay Pack

**Industry:** Travel & Hospitality — airlines, hotels, resorts, OTAs, tour operators, vacation rental platforms, cruise lines, and travel agencies.
**Overlay for base families:** B2C, B2B
**Primary marketing challenges:**
- Seasonal demand volatility: demand spikes around holidays, school breaks, and seasonal events — marketing must accelerate early booking while maintaining visibility through off-peak periods
- Review-gate purchase behavior: travelers consult reviews before booking; trust signals (star ratings, traveler count, UGC) are purchase prerequisites, not enhancements
- Aspiration-to-booking gap: the traveler's imagination is their first purchase — content must close the gap between aspiration ("I want to go somewhere") and concrete booking confidence ("I'm booking this specific property/package now")

**Disciplines covered:** Paid_Media · Content_SEO · Lifecycle_Email · Social · Landing_Pages

## When to use this overlay
Use for destination marketing, hospitality properties, travel packages, OTA campaigns, tour operators, and travel agencies marketing leisure or business travel products to end consumers or travel-purchasing business buyers. This overlay adds travel-specific aspiration framing, review-gate trust architecture, and seasonal urgency signals on top of the selected base family pack.
