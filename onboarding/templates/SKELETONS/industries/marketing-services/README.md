# Marketing Services Industry Overlay Pack

**Industry:** Marketing Services — marketing agencies, digital marketing agencies, performance marketing firms, media buying agencies, and growth consultancies.
**Overlay for base families:** Agency, B2B
**Primary marketing challenges:**
- Performance accountability: clients measure agencies on ROAS, CPL, and pipeline contribution — marketing for the agency itself must demonstrate the same metrics fluency
- Commoditization: the space is crowded with generic "full-service digital marketing" positioning — differentiation requires demonstrably unique methodology or proof of superior outcomes
- CMO skepticism: senior marketing buyers have seen every agency pitch; they respond to specificity and results, not aspiration and process diagrams

**Disciplines covered:** Paid_Media · Content_SEO · Lifecycle_Email · Social · Landing_Pages

## When to use this overlay
Use for digital marketing agencies, performance marketing firms, media buying agencies, growth consultancies, and in-house agency teams marketing their services to external clients. This overlay adds performance-metrics culture, CMO-buying-context framing, and data-driven proof posture on top of the selected base family pack.
